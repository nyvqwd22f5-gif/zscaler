# Enabling or Disabling Scan Types in Credential Exposure Scan
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/itdr/enabling-or-disabling-scan-types-credential-exposure-scan
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1531762.pdf

After you [configure a credential exposure scan](/itdr/configuring-endpoint-credential-exposure-scan) on the endpoint agents to identify credential vulnerabilities and potential privilege escalation attack paths, you can edit the scan configuration to enable or disable an [exposure or a scan type](/itdr/viewing-endpoint-credential-exposure-issues). After you save the configuration, only the enabled exposure or scan types run on the endpoints to detect credential vulnerabilities.
By default, all exposure or scan types are enabled.
To enable or disable the exposure or scan type:
- Go to **ITDR** > **Manage** > **Endpoint Credential Exposure**.
-
Locate the scan you want to enable or disable the exposure or scan types for, and click the **Edit **icon.
[See image.](#itdr-scan-type-edit-icon-image)
-
In the configuration window, select **Scans**.
[See image.](#itdr-scans-option-image)
-
Enable or disable scan types as necessary.
[See image.](#itdr-scan-type-enable-disable-image)
-
Click **Save**.
A confirmation message appears indicating that the configuration is saved.
