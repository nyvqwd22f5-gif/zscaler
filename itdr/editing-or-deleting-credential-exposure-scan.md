# Editing or Deleting a Credential Exposure Scan
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/itdr/editing-or-deleting-credential-exposure-scan
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1531691.pdf

You can edit or delete a [credential exposure scan](/itdr/configuring-endpoint-credential-exposure-scan) that you configured.
Editing a Credential Exposure Scan
To edit a credential exposure scan:
- Go to **ITDR **> **Manage** > **Endpoint Credential Exposure**.
-
Locate the endpoint you want to edit, and click the **Edit** icon.
[See image.](#itdr-credential-scan-edit-icon)
-
In the configuration window, modify the details as necessary. You can modify:
- [Configuration](/itdr/configuring-endpoint-credential-exposure-scan)
- [Endpoint Selection](/itdr/specifying-selection-criterion-credential-exposure-scan)
[See image.](#itdr-credential-scan-modify-details)
- Click **Save**.
Deleting a Credential Exposure Scan
To delete a credential exposure scan:
- Go to **ITDR **> **Manage** > **Endpoint Credential Exposure**.
-
Locate the endpoint you want to delete, and click the **Delete **icon.
[See image.](#itdr-credential-scan-delete-icon)
- In the confirmation window, click **OK**.
After you delete a scan, all the identity vulnerability results pertaining to that credential exposure on the [Endpoint Credential Exposure](/itdr/about-endpoint-credential-exposure-dashboard) dashboard are deleted.
