# Editing or Deleting a Notification
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/itdr/editing-or-deleting-notification
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1531785.pdf

You can edit or delete a notification configured in the Zscaler ITDR Admin Portal.
Editing a Notification
To edit a notification:
- Go to **ITDR** > **Notifications** > **Configure**.
- Locate the notification that you want to edit.
-
Click the **Edit** icon for the notification under the **Actions** column.
[See image.](#itdr-notification-edit-icon)
-
In the **Configure Notifications** window, modify the fields as required.
[See image.](#itdr-notification-edit-details)
- Click **Save**.
Deleting a Notification
To delete a notification:
- Go to **ITDR** > **Notifications** > **Configure**.
- Locate the notification that you want to delete.
-
Click the **Delete** icon for the notification under the **Actions** column.
[See image.](#itdr-notification-delete-icon)
- In the confirmation window, click **OK**.
