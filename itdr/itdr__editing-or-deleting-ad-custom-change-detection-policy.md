# Editing or Deleting an Active Directory Custom Change Detection Policy
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/itdr/editing-or-deleting-ad-custom-change-detection-policy
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1531804.pdf

You can edit or delete Active Directory Custom Change Detection policies from the Zscaler ITDR Admin Portal.
Editing a Policy
To edit a policy:
- Go to **ITDR** > **Manage **> **Active Directory** **Change Detection**.
-
Locate the policy that you want to edit, and click the **Edit **icon under the **Actions **column.
[See image.](#edit-policy)
- Make changes to the policy as required.
The AD domain is monitored based on the changes made to the policy.
Deleting a Policy
To delete a policy:
- Go to **ITDR** > **Manage **> **Active Directory** **Change Detection**.
-
Locate the policy that you want to delete, and click the **Delete **icon under the **Actions **column.
[See image.](#delete-policy)
-
Confirm your action.
The policy is removed from the ITDR Admin Portal.
