# Editing or Deleting a Rule
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/itdr/editing-or-deleting-rule
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1531813.pdf

You can edit or delete an orchestration rule that you configured in the Zscaler ITDR Admin Portal.
Editing a Rule
To edit a rule:
- Go to **Orchestrate** > **Rules**.
-
Locate the rule that you want to modify, and click the **Edit** icon under the **Actions **column.
[See image.](#deception-edit-rule-icon-1)
-
In the **Rule Details** window, modify the required fields.
[See image.](#deception-modify-rule-details)
- Click **Save**.
Deleting a Rule
To delete a rule:
- Go to **Orchestrate** > **Rules**.
-
Locate the rule that you want to delete, and click the **Delete **icon under the **Actions **column.
[See image.](#deception-rules-delete-icon)
- In the confirmation window, click **OK**.
