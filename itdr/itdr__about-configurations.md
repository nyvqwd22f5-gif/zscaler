# About Configurations
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/itdr/about-configurations
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1531872.pdf

Zscaler ITDR seamlessly integrates with third-party IT Service Management (ITSM) tools, such as ServiceNow, to track and assess posture and change issues. ITSM integration provides real-time visibility into issues, enabling organizations to take immediate action.
ITSM integration provides the following benefits and enables you to:
- Gain real-time visibility into incidents.
- Assign the incident to appropriate teams or individuals.
- Track and assess the issues.
About the Configurations Page
On the Configurations page (ITDR > Integrations > Configurations), you can do the following:
- View the list of available ITSM integrations. For each ITSM tool, you can view:
- **Enabled**: If the integration is enabled or disabled. The **X **icon indicates that the integration is disabled, and the check mark icon indicates that the integration is enabled.
- **Settings**: The ITSM tool configured.
- [Configure or edit the ITSM integration](/itdr/configuring-itdr-servicenow).
