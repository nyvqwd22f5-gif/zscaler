# About Account Settings
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/itdr/about-account-settings
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1531671.pdf

The **Account Settings** icon (![Icon for Advanced Settings](/downloads/deception/deceive/network-decoys/creating-internal-network-decoy/zscaler-deception-advanced-settings-icon-new.png)
) on the Zscaler ITDR navigation menu enables you to manage your user profile and notification preferences.
Account Settings provide the following benefits and enable you to:
- Customize user profile information such as name and time zone.
- Change the password or reset the 2FA that you used to log in to the Zscaler ITDR Admin Portal.
- Customize your notification preferences.
About the Account Settings Page
On the Account Settings page, you can do the following:
-
Profile: [Reset 2FA](/itdr/resetting-two-factor-authentication), [edit your user profile](/itdr/editing-user-profile)[, and ][change your password](/itdr/changing-your-password).
The **Reset 2FA** and **Change Password** actions are available only if the **Disable Password Login** option is selected while [adding a user](/itdr/about-users-roles#itdr-add-new-user-admin-portal).
- Notification Preferences: Customize your email notification preferences for information relating to the license and security alerts.
