# Editing or Deleting Threat Detection Policies
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/itdr/editing-or-deleting-threat-detection-policies
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1531684.pdf

You can edit or delete a threat detection policy that you [configured](/itdr/creating-endpoint-policy) in the Zscaler ITDR Admin Portal.
Any changes made to a threat detection policy can take a few hours to reflect on the endpoints.
Editing a Threat Detection Policy
To edit a threat detection policy:
- Go to **ITDR** > **Manage** > **Threat Detection**.
-
Locate the threat detection policy you want to edit, and click the **Edit **icon.
[See image.](#edit-policy)
-
In the policy window, modify the policy configuration per your requirements. You can modify:
- [Policy Details](/itdr/creating-endpoint-policy)
- [Endpoint Selection](/itdr/specifying-selection-criterion)
- [Active Directory](/itdr/configuring-itdr-active-directory)
[See image.](#threat-detection-policy-details)
- Click **Save**.
Deleting a Threat Detection Policy
To delete a threat detection policy:
- Go to **ITDR** > **Manage** > **Threat Detection**.
-
Locate the threat detection policy you want to delete, and click the **Delete **icon.
[See image.](#delete-policy)
-
Confirm your action when prompted.
A confirmation message appears indicating that the policy was deleted successfully.
Deleting Selected Threat Detection Policies
To delete selected threat detection policies:
- Go to **ITDR** > **Manage** > **Threat Detection**.
- Select the checkboxes next to the threat detection policies you want to delete.
-
Click **Delete** **Selected**.
[See image.](#view_selecting_a_policy)
-
In the confirmation window, click **OK**.
[See image.](#view_delete_confirmation_window)
The selected threat detection policies are deleted.
