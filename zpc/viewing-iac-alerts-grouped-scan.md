# Viewing IaC Alerts Grouped by Scan
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zpc/viewing-iac-alerts-grouped-scan
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1440686.pdf

The IaC alerts include detailed information about the incident, security policy, and much more. These alerts are also grouped by scan, so you can view details for a specific IaC scan.
To view the IaC alerts grouped by scan:
- In the left-side navigation, select **Alerts**.
- On the **Alerts** page, click the **IaC Alerts** tab.
- Click the **Alert Grouped By Scan** tab.
[See image.](#iacalert-scantab)
- Click the **Scan ID** to view:
- **Scan Details**: The repository details, name of the developer who performed the scan, and the date and time when the scan was performed.
- **Alert Details** The alert, policy, and scan details. To learn more, see [Alert Attributes](/zpc/alert-attributes).
[See image.](#scandrawer)
