# Creating a New Benchmark
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zpc/creating-new-benchmark
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1406936.pdf

Zscaler Posture Control (ZPC) offers creating custom benchmarks and measures compliance posture. You can choose from 300+ security policies and add to your custom benchmarks to cater for your organization's specific compliance needs. To create a custom benchmark:
- Go to **Cloud Posture** > **Compliance**.
- Click **Create Benchmark**.
- Enter the **Benchmark Name**.
- (Optional) Enter the **Description**.
- Filter your cloud infrastructure for benchmarks you want checked:
- **Business Units**: Select one or multiple business units. Business units are logical groups of multiple cloud accounts.
- **Clouds**: Select one or multiple cloud service providers.
- **Accounts**: Select one or multiple cloud accounts.
- In the **Selected Policies** pane, you need to add categories before moving security policies from the **Available Policies** pane:
- Click **Add Category**.
- Enter the **Category Name**, then click **Save**. The new category appears in the **Selected Policies** pane.
[See image.](#selected-policies-gif)
- In the **Available Policies** pane, you can:
- Filter security policies by severity and benchmark name.
- Select security policies to move to the selected policies pane.
- Click **Add To**, then select a policy category. The security policies appear under the selected policy category in the **Selected Policies** pane.
[See image.](#available-policies-gif)
- Click **Save**.
