# Viewing the Compliance Dashboard
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zpc/viewing-compliance-dashboard
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1394196.pdf

The Compliance Dashboard provides insight into your cloud infrastructure's compliance posture. Most dashboard widgets and icons are interactive. You can drill down to view the Compliance Dashboard in more detail.
On the Compliance Dashboard page (Dashboard > Compliance), you can do the following:
- Filter compliance information using available parameters. To learn more, see [Using Filters](/zpc/using-filters#compliance-filters).
- View the following compliance trend for your cloud infrastructure:
- **Policy Compliance**: View the passed and failed policy trend for the selected benchmark and selected duration (last 7 days, last 15 days, last 30 days, last 60 days, last 90 days).
- **Asset Compliance**: View the passed and failed asset trend for the selected benchmark and selected duration (last 7 days, last 15 days, last 30 days, last 60 days, last 90 days).
- View **Security Benchmark Compliance**: View benchmark title, policy count, and compliance score for each security compliance benchmark.
- View **Laws & Regulations Benchmark Compliance**: View benchmark title, policy count, and compliance score for each law & regulation benchmark.
- View **Industry Benchmark Compliance**: View benchmark title, policy count, and compliance score for each industry compliance benchmark.
- View **Custom Benchmark**: View benchmark title, policy count, and compliance score for each custom benchmark.
