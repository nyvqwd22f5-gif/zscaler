# Managing Executive Reports
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zpc/managing-executive-reports
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1457326.pdf

You can view, edit, or delete an executive report that you've customized and saved, when required.
Viewing Saved Executive Reports
To view the saved executive reports:
- Go to **Analytics **> **Report Management**.
- On the **Report Management** page, click the ** Saved Reports** tab.
A list of saved executive reports is displayed.
- Search for a specific executive report.
- Select the required columns that must be shown in the table. To learn more, see [Using Tables](/zpc/using-tables).
[![Manage Saved Executive Reports](/downloads/zpc/analytics/managing-executive-reports/zpc-saved-executive-report.png)
]
Editing an Executive Report
To edit an executive report:
- Go to **Analytics **> **Report Management**.
- On the **Report Management** page, click the ** Saved Reports** tab.
A list of saved executive reports is displayed.
- In the **Saved Reports **table, click the report name that you want to edit.
[See image.](#edit)
The customized report is displayed.
- Click **Customize View** and select or deselect the required parameters.
- Click the **Save** icon to view the following options:
-
**Save as New Report:** Click to save the edited report as another customized report.
-
In the **Save New Report** window, enter a name for this report.
-
Click **Save**.
[See image.](#zpc-save-executive-report)
-
**Save Changes:** Click to save the report.
[See image](#zpc-save-changes)
A confirmation message appears indicating that the report was updated successfully.
Deleting an Executive Report
To delete an executive report:
- Go to **Analytics **> **Report Management**.
- On the **Report Management** page, click the ** Saved Reports** tab.
A list of saved executive reports is displayed.
- Click the **Delete** icon for the executive report that you want to delete.
- A confirmation message appears. Click **Confirm**.
[See image.](#zpc-executive-report-delete-confirm)
The executive report is deleted.
