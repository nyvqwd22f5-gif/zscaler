# Editing or Deleting a Business Unit
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zpc/editing-deleting-business-unit
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1408296.pdf

You can edit or delete a business unit when required.
Editing a Business Unit
- Go to **Administration** > **Business Unit Management**.
- On the **Business Unit Management** page, click the **Edit** icon for the business unit that you want to edit.
[See image.](#bu-editicon)
- In the **Edit Business Unit** window, change the **Name** or select a different **Group** from the drop-down menu.
[See image.](#bu-edit)
- Click **Save**.
Deleting a Business Unit
To delete a business unit:
- Go to **Administration** > **Business Unit Management**.
- On the **Business Unit Management** page, click the **Delete** icon for the business unit that you want to delete.
[See image.](#bu-deleteicon)
- In the **Delete Business Unit** window, click **Delete**.
[See image.](#bu-deleteoption)
If any cloud account is associated with the business unit, then you must move the cloud account to another business unit. Otherwise, ZPC moves the cloud account to a default business unit. [See image.](#bu-delete)
