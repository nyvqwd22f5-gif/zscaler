# Editing or Deleting Administrator Accounts
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zpc/editing-deleting-administrator-accounts
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1396871.pdf

Administrators with the required permissions can edit or delete other administrator accounts. However, while each administrator can edit their account, they cannot delete their own account.
Editing an Administrator Account
To edit an administrator account:
- Go to **Administration **> **Administrators and Groups**.
- On the **Administrators and Groups** page, do one of the following:
- Click the **Edit** icon for the administrator that you want to edit.
[See image.](#editicon)
- Click the **Administrator Name** to open the admin drawer, then click the **Edit** icon.
[See image.](#drawer-edit)
- In the **Edit Administrator **window, change the **Name**, **Email ID**, **Group**, or reset the password. When you click **Reset Password**, the administrator receives an email to reset their password.
[See image.](#editwindow)
- Click **Save**.
A message appears indicating the administrator account has been updated successfully.
Deleting an Administrator Account
To delete an administrator account:
- Go to **Administration **> **Administrators and Groups**.
- Click the **Delete** icon for the administrator that you want to delete or click the **Administrator Name** to open the admin drawer and click the **Delete** icon.
- In the **Delete Administrator** window, click **Delete**.
[See image.](#delete)
A message appears indicating the administrator account has been deleted successfully.
