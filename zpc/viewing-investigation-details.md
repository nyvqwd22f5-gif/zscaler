# Viewing Investigation Details
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zpc/viewing-investigation-details
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1443496.pdf

Each saved investigation has an Investigation Details drawer that shows the investigation query structure. In the Investigation Details drawer, you can view, edit, run, or delete saved investigations.
To view the Investigations Details drawer:
- From the **Investigation **page, click the **Saved **tab.
- Click the investigation **Name **or **Query**. The **Investigation Details **drawer is displayed.
- On the **Investigation Details **drawer, you can view the saved investigation query details. You can also run, edit, or delete the query using the icons in the upper right.
