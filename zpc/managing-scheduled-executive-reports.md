# Managing Scheduled Executive Reports
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zpc/managing-scheduled-executive-reports
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1462836.pdf

You can view, edit, or delete a scheduled executive report, when required.
Viewing Scheduled Executive Reports
To view scheduled executive reports:
- Go to **Analytics **> **Report Management**.
- On the **Report Management** page, click the **Scheduled Reports** tab.
A list of scheduled executive reports is displayed.
- Search for a specific scheduled executive report.
- Select the required columns that must be shown in the table. To learn more, see [Using Tables](/zpc/using-tables).
![View the Scheduled Executive Reports](/downloads/zpc/analytics/about-executive-report/zpc-scheduled-reports-tab.png)
Editing a Scheduled Executive Report
- Go to **Analytics **> **Report Management**.
- On the **Report Management** page, click the **Scheduled Reports** tab.
- Click the **Edit** icon for the scheduled executive report that you want to edit.
[See image.](#edit-icon)
- In the **Edit Scheduled Report** window, change the **Schedule Name**, **Report**, **Recipients**, **Status**, or **Frequency** as required.
[See image.](#edit-window)
- Click **Save**.
Deleting a Scheduled Executive Report
- Go to **Analytics **> **Report Management**.
- On the **Report Management** page, click the **Scheduled Reports** tab.
- Click the **Delete** icon for the scheduled executive report that you want to delete.
[See image.](#delete-icon)
- A confirmation message appears. Click **Delete**.
[See image.](#delete-report)
A message appears indicating that the schedule report is deleted successfully.
