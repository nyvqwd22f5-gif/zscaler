# Adding a Business Unit
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zpc/adding-business-unit
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1403181.pdf

You can add business units and assign them to a single group or multiple groups. Users in the [group](/zpc/about-groups) can view data and perform tasks on cloud accounts within the assigned business units.
To add a business unit:
- Go to **Administration** > **Business Unit Management**.
- On the** Business Unit Management** page, click **Add Business Unit**.
[See image.](#bu-page)
- In the **Add Business Unit** window:
- **Name**: Enter a name for the business unit.
- **Assign To Groups**: Select the required groups from the drop-down menu.
[See image.](#bu-window)
By default, the "Default Super Admin" and "Default Read Only" predefined groups are assigned to all business units.
[See image.](#predefined)
- Click **Create**.
The business unit is added and displayed on the **Business Unit Management** page.
