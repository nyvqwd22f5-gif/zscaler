# Editing or Deleting Scheduled Reports
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zpc/editing-or-deleting-scheduled-reports
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1436466.pdf

You can edit or delete a reporting schedule when required.
Editing a Scheduled Report
To edit a reporting schedule:
- Go to **Administration** > **Scheduled Reports**.
- Click the **Edit** icon for the scheduled report that you want to edit.
- Click the **Edit **icon for the fields you want to edit.
[See image.](#editreport)
- Click **Next**.
- Click **Finish**.
A message appears indicating that the schedule is updated successfully.
Deleting a Scheduled Report
To delete a reporting schedule:
- Go to **Administration** > **Scheduled Reports**.
- Click the **Delete** icon for the scheduled report that you want to delete.
- A confirmation message appears. Click **Delete**.
[See image.](#delete)
A message appears indicating that the schedule is deleted successfully.
