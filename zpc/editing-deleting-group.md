# Editing or Deleting a Group
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zpc/editing-deleting-group
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1396786.pdf

You can modify the group details and assign a different role and different set of business units to the group. All users in that group are assigned a new role and can view data or perform tasks on cloud accounts within the assigned business units.
Editing a Group
To edit a group:
- Go to **Administration **>** Administrators and Groups**.
- On the **Administrators and Groups** page, select the **Groups** tab.
- Click the **Edit** icon for the group that you want to edit.
[See image.](#edit-button)
- In the **Edit Group** window, change the **Group Name**,** Role**, **Scope**, enable or disable **Single Sign-On**, or update the **Comments** as required.
[See image.](#edit-group)
- Click **Save**.
A message appears indicating that the group has been updated successfully.
Deleting a Group
To delete a group:
- Go to **Administration **> **Administrators and Groups**.
- On the **Administrators and Groups** page, select the **Groups** tab.
[See image. ](#delete-icon)
- Click the **Delete** icon for the group that you want to delete.
- In the **Delete Group** window, click **Delete**.
[See image.](#message)
If administrators are present in a group, then you need to move the administrators to other groups and then delete the selected group.
- In the **Delete Group** window, click **Move Admins**.
[See image.](#moveadmin)
- In the **Move Administrators** window, select the required groups from the drop-down menu.
[See image.](#select)
- Click **Save**.
A message appears indicating that the administrators have been moved to the new groups successfully.
