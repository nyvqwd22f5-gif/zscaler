# Viewing Alerts Grouped by Policy
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zpc/viewing-alerts-grouped-policy
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1433981.pdf

You can view cloud alerts and IaC alerts that are by [security policies](/zpc/viewing-security-policy-details). You can also view detailed information about the policy.
Viewing Cloud Alerts Grouped by Policy
- In the left-side navigation, click **Alerts**.
- On the **Alerts** page, click the **Cloud Alerts **tab.
- Click the **Alerts Grouped by Policy** tab.
[See image.](#cloudalertstab)
- Click the **Policy Name** to view the **Policy **and **Alert** details. To learn more about each alert attribute, see [Alert Attributes](/zpc/alert-attributes).
[See image.](#cloudpolicy)
Viewing IaC Alerts Grouped by Policy
- In the left-side navigation, click **Alerts**.
- On the **Alerts** page, click the **IaC Alerts **tab.
- Click the **Alerts Grouped by Policy** tab.
[See image.](#iacalertstab)
- Click the **Policy Name** to view the **Policy**, **Compliance**, and **Alert** details. To learn more about each alert attribute, see [Alert Attributes](/zpc/alert-attributes).
[See image.](#iacscanalerts)
