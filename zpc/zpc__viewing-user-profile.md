# Viewing the User Profile
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zpc/viewing-user-profile
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1404541.pdf

Zscaler Posture Control (ZPC) configures a profile for your user account and displays the details on the My Profiles page.
To view your profile:
- Click the **Profile** ( ![Click the profile icon](/downloads/zpc/getting-started/admin-portal/viewing-user-profile/zpc-profile-icon.png)
) icon on the left navigation menu.
- On the **My Profiles** page, you can see the following details:
- **Email Address**: Your registered email address. Click the **Edit** icon to change your email address if required.
- **Group**: The group to which your account belongs.
- **Role**: The role associated with the group.
- **Password**: Click the **Edit** icon to reset your password.
- **My Tenants**: The tenants that are assigned to your account.
- **My Downloads**: View the list of reports you've requested to download. To learn more, see [Downloading Reports](/zpc/downloading-reports).
