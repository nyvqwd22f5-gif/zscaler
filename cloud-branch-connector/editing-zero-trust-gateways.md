# Editing Zero Trust Gateways
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/cloud-branch-connector/editing-zero-trust-gateways
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1529794.pdf

Modifying Zscaler Zero Trust Gateway groups or viewing individual Zero Trust Gateways are some of the tasks you can complete after deployment.
To edit or view a Zero Trust Gateway:
- Go to **Administration** > **Cloud Connector Groups**.
-
On the [Cloud Connector Groups](/cloud-branch-connector/about-cloud-connector-groups) page, locate the Zero Trust Gateway group or individual Zero Trust Gateway you want to modify and click the **Edit** or **View** icon.
[See image.](#editicon)
- [Zero Trust Gateway group](#ztgwgroup)
- [Individual Zero Trust Gateway](#ztgw)
[]
- In the **Edit Connectors** window, you can configure or view the following:
- In the **Tunnel Information** section, under **ZIA Tunnel Mode**, select **Unencrypted UDP** or **DTLS**.
-
In the **Group Information** section, you can view:
- **Name**: The name of the Zero Trust Gateway group.
- **Location Name**: The [location](/cloud-branch-connector/about-locations) from where your Zero Trust Gateway group was deployed.
[See image.](#ztgwgroupdetails)
- Click **Save** and [activate the change](/cloud-branch-connector/saving-and-activating-changes).
[]
In the **Edit Connectors** window, you can view the following:
- In the **Tunnel Information** section, the **ZIA Tunnel Mode** set as **Unencrypted UDP** or **DTLS**.
-
In the **Group Information** section:
- **Name**: The name of the Zero Trust Gateway.
- **Location Name**: The [location](/cloud-branch-connector/about-locations) from where your Zero Trust Gateway was deployed.
[See image.](#ztgwdetails)
