# Managing User Profile
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zidentity/managing-user-profile
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1499176.pdf

You can view your admin account details (name, login ID and email address) on the My Profile page. You can edit these details when required.
To access the My Profile page:
-
On the ZIdentity Landing Page, click the **My Profile** icon in the top right corner.
[See image.](#zsl-selectprofile)
Your account details are displayed.
[See image.](#zsl-profilepage)
- You can do the following:
- [Edit Full Name](#name)
- [Edit Email Address](#email)
- [Change Password](#password)
- [Change Language or Time Zone](#language-time)
- []Click the **Edit** icon next to your name.
- Edit your name.
-
Click the **Check** icon.
[See image.](#zid-editfullname)
- []Click the **Edit** icon next to your email address.
- In the **Edit Email Address** window:
- **Enter New Email Address**: Enter your new email address and click **Validate Email**. A validation code is sent to the email address entered.
- **Enter Validation Code**: Enter the validation code sent to your new email address. If you don't receive the code, click **Resend Code** to send a new validation code to the email address.
- Click **Verify**. The email address is validated.
- Click **Done**.
You can also edit the email address in the ZIdentity Admin Portal. Click **My Profile **in the** **left-side navigation to edit the email address.
-
[]Click **Change Password**.
The **Change Password** window appears.
- In the **Change Password** window:
- **Current Password**: Enter your current password used for logging in to the ZIdentity Admin Portal.
- **New Password**: Enter a new password. The password must have at least a minimum of 8 characters, 1 lowercase character (a-z), 1 uppercase character (A-Z), 1 numeric character (0-9), and 1 special character.
- **Confirm New Password**: Retype the password to confirm.
- Click **Reset**.
You can also change the password in the ZIdentity Admin Portal. Click **My Profile **in the** **left-side navigation to change the password.
- []Click the **Language** or **Time Zone** drop-down menu.
- Select a language or time zone.
- Click **Apply**.
