# Accessing and Navigating the ZIdentity Landing Page
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zidentity/accessing-and-navigating-zidentity-landing-page
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1499046.pdf

This article covers the following topics:
- [Accessing the ZIdentity Landing Page](#access-portal)
- [Navigating within the ZIdentity Landing Page](#navigating-portal)
[]Your admin configures your ZIdentity account, provides access to tasks as per your role, and emails you for [configuring multi-factor authentication (MFA)](/zidentity/configuring-mfa).
All users are required to set up MFA for improved security.
After configuring your authentication preference, you can sign in to the ZIdentity Landing Page by using one of the following methods:
- [Password](#password)
- [Security Key or Biometric](#security-key-biometric)
-
[]Enter your **Login ID **and select **Remember me **if you want the service to remember your login ID the next time you log in.
[See image.](#zsl-loginid)
- Click** Next**.
-
Enter your **Password **and click **Sign In**.
[See image.](#Password-auth)
- Based on your MFA configuration, complete the two-factor authentication (2FA).
- []Enter your **Login ID **and select **Remember me **if you want the service to remember your login ID the next time you log in.
-
Select **Sign-in using Security Key or Biometric**.
[See image.](#security-key)
- Click** Next**.
- Based on your configuration, enter your security key or complete the biometric.
[]You can access the ZIdentity Admin Portal and the subscribed Zscaler services from the ZIdentity Landing page.
If your organization is enabled for Experience Center, click the **Experience Center** link at the top to go to the Experience Center.
This banner is only visible to ZIdentity tenants who have migrated to Experience Center.
[See image.](#zsl-tiles)
To view or [manage your profile](/zidentity/managing-user-profile), click your name in the top-right corner.
To log out, click the **Logout **icon.
