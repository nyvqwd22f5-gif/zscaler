# About Landmine Decoy Personalities
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/deception/about-landmine-decoy-personalities
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1531532.pdf

Landmine decoy personalities are templates that can be used to create [landmine decoys](/deception/about-landmine-decoys) via [deception strategies](/deception/about-deploy-strategy). Zscaler Deception provides a list of preconfigured landmine decoy personalities for various business use cases. You can also create custom personalities based on your business requirements.
Landmine decoy personalities are created using landmine policies similar to landmine decoys.
Landmine decoy personalities provide the following benefits and enable you to:
- Use landmine policy templates to deploy landmine decoys via a deception strategy.
- Leverage preconfigured landmine policy templates or create new templates to define landmine decoys for various business use cases.
About the Landmine Decoy Personalities Page
On the Landmine Decoy Personalities page (Miragemaker > Strategy Builder > Landmine), you can do the following:
- View the list of preconfigured and custom landmine decoy personalities, and search for a specific entry by name. For each decoy personality, you can see:
- **Name**: The name of the landmine decoy personality.
- **Agentless**: The state of whether the landmine decoy policy can be applied with or without agents. A green checkmark indicates that the policy supports agentless deployment. A red X mark indicates that the policy does not support agentless deployment. You can also filter the landmine decoys based on the agentless support by setting the column filter to `true` or `false`.
- **Selection Criterion**: The criteria used to apply landmine policies that are configured in the landmine decoy personalities.
- **Policies**: The list of landmine policies that are configured in the landmine decoy personalities.
