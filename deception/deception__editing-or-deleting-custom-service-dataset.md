# Editing or Deleting a Custom Service Dataset
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/deception/editing-or-deleting-custom-service-dataset
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1531547.pdf

You can edit or delete custom service datasets that you configured in the Zscaler Deception Admin Portal
Editing a Custom Service Dataset
To edit a custom service dataset:
- Go to **Miragemaker** > **Custom Service Datasets**.
-
Under **Actions**, click the **Edit** icon for the dataset you want to modify.
[See image.](#zd-edit-option-csd)
-
In the **Custom Service Dataset Details** window, modify the [fields as required](/deception/configuring-custom-service-dataset).
[See image.](#zd-edit-window-csd)
- Click **Submit**.
The default custom service datasets cannot be edited.
Deleting a Custom Service Dataset
To delete a custom service dataset:
- Go to **Miragemaker** > **Custom Service Datasets**.
-
Under **Actions**, click the **Delete** icon for the dataset you want to delete.
[See image.](#zd-delete-option-csd)
- In the confirmation window, click **OK**.
The default custom service datasets cannot be deleted.
