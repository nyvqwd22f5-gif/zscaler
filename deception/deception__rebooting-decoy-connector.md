# Rebooting a Decoy Connector
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/deception/rebooting-decoy-connector
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1531415.pdf

You can reboot a Decoy Connector that you have configured in the Zscaler Deception Admin Portal.
To reboot a Decoy Connector:
- Go to **Settings** > **Topology** > **Decoy Connectors.**
-
Select the Decoy Connector you want to reboot, and then click **Actions** > **Reboot**.
[See image.](#actions-reboot-decoy-connector)
- In the confirmation window, click **OK**.
[]![Reboot a Decoy Connector option](/downloads/deception/product-documentation/settings/topology/decoy-connector/decoy-connector-management/rebooting-decoy-connector/zscaler-deception-reboot-decoy-connector-option.png)
