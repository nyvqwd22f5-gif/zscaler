# About Threat Intelligence Decoy Personalities
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/deception/about-threat-intelligence-decoy-personalities
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1531527.pdf

Threat Intelligence (TI) decoy personalities are templates that can be used to create [TI decoys](/deception/about-threat-intelligence-decoys) via [deception strategies](/deception/about-deception-strategy). Zscaler Deception provides a list of preconfigured TI decoy personalities for various business use cases. You can also create custom personalities based on your business requirements.
TI decoy personalities provide the following benefits and enable you to:
- Use templates to deploy TI decoys via deception strategies.
- Leverage preconfigured templates or create new templates to define TI decoys for various business use cases.
About the Threat Intelligence Decoy Personalities Page
On the Threat Intelligence Decoy Personalities page (Miragemaker > Strategy Builder > Threat Intelligence Decoy), you can do the following:
- View the list of preconfigured and custom threat intelligence decoy personalities, and search for a specific entry by name. For each decoy personality, you can see:
- **Name**: The name of the TI decoy personality.
- **Description**: The description of the TI decoy personality.
- [Configure a TI decoy personality](/deception/configuring-threat-intelligence-decoy-personality).
- [Download a TI decoy personality](/deception/downloading-threat-intelligence-decoy-personality).
- [Edit or delete a TI decoy personality](/deception/editing-or-deleting-threat-intelligence-decoy-personality).
- [Export all TI decoy personalities](/deception/exporting-threat-intelligence-decoy-personalities).
