# Viewing ZPA App Connector Update Logs
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/deception/viewing-zpa-app-connector-update-logs
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1531570.pdf

Update logs provide valuable information that you can use to troubleshoot ZPA App Connector update issues.
To view the update logs:
- Go to **Settings** > **Topology** > **ZPA App Connectors**.
- Click **Logs **for an App Connector to view the update logs.
-
Select **View update logs** from the drop-down menu.
[See image.](#deception-zpa-app-connector-view-update-logs)
-
When the **Update Logs** window appears, view the logs, and then click **Close**.
[See image.](#deception-app-conn-update-logs-list)
