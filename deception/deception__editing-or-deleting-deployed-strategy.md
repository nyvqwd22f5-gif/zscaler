# Editing or Deleting a Deployed Strategy
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/deception/editing-or-deleting-deployed-strategy
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1531468.pdf

You can edit or delete a deployed strategy in the Zscaler Deception Admin Portal.
Editing a Deployed Strategy
To edit a deployed strategy:
- Go to **Deceive** > **Deploy Strategy**.
- Under **Actions**, click the **Edit** icon for the deployed strategy that you want to modify.
[See image.](#deception-edit-deployed-strategy-icon)
- In the **Deploy Network Strategy** window, modify the required fields on [each tab](/deception/deploying-strategy), and then click **Next**.
[See image.](#deception-strategy-modify-details)
- Click **Create Decoys **or **Create Decoys & Deploy**.
[See image.](#deception-strategy-modify-create-deploy)
Deleting a Deployed Strategy
To delete a deployed strategy:
- Go to **Deceive** > **Deploy Strategy**.
- Under **Actions**, click the **Delete** icon for the deployed strategy that you want to delete.
[See image.](#deception-strategy-delete)
- In the confirmation window, click **OK**.
