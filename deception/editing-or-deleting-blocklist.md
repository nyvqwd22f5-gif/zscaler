# Editing or Deleting Blocklists
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/deception/editing-or-deleting-blocklist
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1531585.pdf

You can edit or delete blocklists that you configured in the Zscaler Deception Admin Portal.
Editing a Blocklist
To edit a blocklist:
- Go to **Deceive** > **Deceive Settings** > **Blocklist**.
-
In the table, locate the blocklist you want to modify, and then click the **Edit** icon.
[See image.](#zd-edit-blocklist)
-
Modify the fields as necessary.
[See image.](#zd-modify-blocklist)
- Click **Save**.
Deleting a Blocklist
To delete a blocklist:
- Go to **Deceive** > **Deceive Settings** > **Blocklist**.
-
In the table, locate the blocklist you want to delete, and then click the **Delete** icon.
[See image.](#zd-delete-blocklist)
- In the confirmation window, click **OK**.
Deleting Multiple or All Blocklists
To delete mutiple or all blocklists:
- Go to **Deceive** > **Deceive Settings** > **Blocklist**.
- In the table, select blocklists you want to delete.
-
Click **Actions** and choose **Delete** to delete the selected blocklists.
[See image.](#zd-delete-multiple-all-blocklists)
You can click** Delete All** to delete all blocklists.
- In the confirmation window, click **OK**.
