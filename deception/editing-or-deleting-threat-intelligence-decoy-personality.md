# Editing or Deleting a Threat Intelligence Decoy Personality
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/deception/editing-or-deleting-threat-intelligence-decoy-personality
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1531530.pdf

You can edit or delete Threat Intelligence (TI) decoy personalities that you configured in the Zscaler Deception Admin Portal.
Editing a Threat Intelligence Decoy Personality
To edit a TI decoy personality:
- Go to **Miragemaker** > **Strategy Builder **> **Threat Intelligence Decoy**.
- Under **Actions**, click the **Edit** icon for the decoy personality you want to modify.
[See image.](#zd-edit-option-tidp)
- In the **Decoy Details** window, modify the required fields.
[See image.](#zd-edit-window-tidp)
- Click **Save**.
The default TI decoy personalities cannot be edited.
Deleting a Threat Intelligence Decoy Personality
To delete a TI decoy personality:
- Go to **Miragemaker** > **Strategy Builder **> **Threat Intelligence Decoy**.
- Under **Actions**, click the **Delete** icon for the decoy personality you want to delete.
[See image.](#zd-delete-option-tidp)
- In the confirmation window, click **OK**.
The default TI decoy personalities cannot be deleted.
