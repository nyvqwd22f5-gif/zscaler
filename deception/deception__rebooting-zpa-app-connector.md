# Rebooting a ZPA App Connector
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/deception/rebooting-zpa-app-connector
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1531572.pdf

You can reboot a ZPA App Connector that you configured in the Zscaler Deception Admin Portal.
To reboot an App Connector:
- Go to **Settings** > **Topology** > **ZPA App Connectors.**
-
Select the App Connector you want to reboot, and then click **Actions** > **Reboot**.
[See image.](#deception-zpa-app-connector-reboot)
- In the confirmation window, click **OK**.
[]![Reboot a ZPA App Connector](/downloads/deception/settings/topology/zpa-app-connectors/rebooting-zpa-app-connector/zscaler-deception-zpa-app-conn-reboot.png)
