# Editing or Deleting a Strategy
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/deception/editing-or-deleting-deception-strategy
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1531467.pdf

You can edit or delete a strategy that you configured in the Zscaler Deception Admin Portal.
Editing a Strategy
To edit a strategy:
- Go to **Miragemaker **>** Strategy Builder **>** Deception Strategy**.
- Under **Actions**, click the **Edit** icon for the Deception strategy you want to modify.
[See image.](#deception-strategy-edit-icon)
- In the **Strategy Detail** window, modify the required fields on [each tab](/deception/creating-deception-strategy), and then click **Validate**.
[See image.](#deception-strategy-modify-details)
- Click **Save**.
[See image.](#deception-validate-save-stratey)
Deleting a Strategy
To delete a strategy:
- Go to **Miragemaker **>** Strategy Builder **>** Deception Strategy**.
- Under **Actions**, click the **Delete** icon for the Deception strategy you want to delete.
[See image.](#deception-delete-strategy)
- In the confirmation window, click **OK**.
