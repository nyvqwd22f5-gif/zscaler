# Managing Landmine Policies
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/deception/managing-landmine-policies
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1531297.pdf

You can edit or delete a landmine policy that you configured in the Zscaler Deception Admin Portal.
Any changes made to a landmine policy can take a few hours to reflect on the endpoints.
Editing a Landmine Policy
To edit a landmine policy:
- Log in to the Deception Admin Portal.
- In the left-side navigation, go to **Deceive** > **Landmine** > **Policies**.
-
Locate the policy that you want to edit, and click the **Edit** icon.
[See image.](#edit-policy)
- In the policy window, modify the policy configuration per your requirements. You can modify:
- [Password Settings](/deception/configuring-password-settings)
- [Defense Evasion](/deception/configuring-defense-evasion)
- [Privilege Escalation](/deception/configuring-privilege-escalation)
- [Cloud Lures](/deception/configuring-cloud-lures)
- [Browser Lures](/deception/configuring-browser-lures)
- [Session Lures](/deception/configuring-session-lures)
- [File Decoys](/deception/configuring-file-decoys)
- [Lure Settings](/deception/configuring-lure-settings)
- [Advanced Deception Capabilities](/deception/enable-advanced-deception-capabilities)
- Click **Save**.
Deleting Landmine Policies
To delete one or more landmine policies:
- Log in to the Deception Admin Portal.
- In the left-side navigation, go to **Deceive** > **Landmine** > **Policies**.
- On the **Policies **page, do one of the following:
-
To delete a single policy, locate the policy that you want to delete, and click the **Delete** icon.
[See image.](#delete-policy)
-
To delete multiple policies, select the policies that you want to delete, and click **Delete Selected**.
[See image.](#delete-seleted)
-
In the confirmation window, click **OK**.
[See image.](#confirm-deletion-policy)
