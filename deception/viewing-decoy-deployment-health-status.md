# Viewing Decoy Deployment Health Status
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/deception/viewing-decoy-deployment-health-status
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1531351.pdf

You can view the deployment health status of all the Decoy Connector and service back ends from the Deceive Summary page. You can view the following health statuses:
- Ready for deployment: All the Decoy Connectors and service back ends are up and running.
- Action Required: Some or all Decoy Connectors and service back ends are down and need your attention.
To view the deployment health status:
- Go to **Deceive** > **Summary**.
- Click the deployment health status button. For example, click **Ready for Deployment.**
[See image.](#deception-ready-for-deployment-option)
The deployment health status of the Decoy Connector and service back ends appears.
[See image.](#deception-deployment-health-status)
- Click **Done**.
