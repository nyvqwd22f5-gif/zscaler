# Editing or Deleting a ThreatParse Rule
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/deception/editing-or-deleting-threatparse-rule
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1531550.pdf

You can edit or delete ThreatParse Rules that you configured in the Zscaler Deception Admin Portal.
Editing a ThreatParse Rule
To edit a ThreatParse rule:
- Go to **Miragemaker** > **ThreatParse Rules**.
-
Under **Actions**, click the **Edit** icon for the rule you want to modify.
[See image.](#zd-edit-option-tpr)
-
In the **Rule Details** window, modify the [required fields](/deception/configuring-threatparse-rule).
[See image.](#zd-edit-window-tpr)
- Click **Submit**.
The default ThreatParse rules cannot be edited.
Deleting a ThreatParse Rule
To delete a ThreatParse rule:
- Go to **Miragemaker** > **ThreatParse**.
-
Under **Actions**, click the **Delete** icon for the rule you want to delete.
[See image.](#zd-delete-option-tpr)
- In the confirmation window, click **OK**.
The default ThreatParse rules cannot be deleted.
