# Editing or Deleting a Safe Process
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/deception/editing-or-deleting-safe-process
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1531456.pdf

You can edit or delete a safe process that you configured in the Zscaler Deception Admin Portal.
Editing a Safe Process
To edit a safe process:
- Go to **Settings **> **Endpoint Settings **> **Safe Processes**.
-
In the table, select the safe process you want to modify, and then click the **Edit** icon.
[See image.](#deception-safe-process-edit-icon)
-
In the **Safe Process Details** window, modify the fields as necessary.
[See image.](#deception-safe-process-edit-details)
- Click **Save**.
For internal safe processes, only the option to enable or disable is supported.
Deleting a Safe Process
To delete a safe process:
- Go to **Settings **> **Endpoint Settings **> **Safe Processes**.
-
In the table, select the safe process you want to delete, and then click the **Delete** icon.
[See image.](#deception-safe-process-delete-icon)
- In the confirmation window, click **OK**.
Internal safe processes cannot be deleted.
