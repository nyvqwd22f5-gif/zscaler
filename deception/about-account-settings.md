# About Account Settings
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zia/about-account-settings
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1402041.pdf

Account settings allow you to manage your account and customize various aspects of your account. You can modify or view your account settings from the Settings** **page in the Zscaler Management Portal for Partners.
Account settings provide the following benefits and enable you to:
- Update and manage your account details such as email address, contact information, logo, reset password etc.
- Manage subscription agreements, notifications and authentication.
About the Settings Page
On the Settings page, you can do the following:
- View your account information:
- The partner account name.
- **Partner ID**: The ID generated when you created your account with Zscaler.
- The status of your account.
- **Partner Type**: The type of partner account.
- [Update your account logo.](/zia/updating-partner-account-logo)
- [Modify your account settings.](/zia/modifying-your-account-settings)
