# About Account Settings
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/deception/about-account-settings
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1531264.pdf

The **Account Settings** icon (![Icon for Account Settings](/downloads/deception/deceive/network-decoys/creating-internal-network-decoy/zscaler-deception-advanced-settings-icon-new.png)
) in the left-side navigation enables you to manage your user profile and notification preferences.
Account Settings provide the following benefits and enable you to:
- Customize user profile information such as name and time zone.
- Change the password or reset the 2FA that you used to log in to the Zscaler Deception Admin Portal.
- Customize your notification preferences.
About the Account Settings Page
On the Account Settings page, you can do the following:
-
Profile: [Reset 2FA](/deception/resetting-two-factor-authentication), [edit your user profile](/deception/editing-user-profile), and [change your password](/deception/changing-password).
The **Reset 2FA** and **Change Password** actions are available only if the **Disable Password Login** option is selected while [adding a user](/deception/about-users-roles#deception-add-new-user-admin-portal).
- Notification Preferences: Customize your email notification preferences for information relating to the license, security alerts, system health, and Zero Trust.
