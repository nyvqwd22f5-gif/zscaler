# Editing or Deleting a Decoy Connector
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/deception/editing-or-deleting-decoy-connector
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1531414.pdf

You can edit or delete a Decoy Connector that you have configured in the Zscaler Deception Admin Portal.
Zscaler Deception uses Zscaler Private Access (ZPA) or Threat Intelligence Decoy Connectors to deploy decoys in a dedicated Deception cloud. You cannot edit or delete them. Contact Zscaler Support to manage them.
Editing a Decoy Connector
To edit a Decoy Connector:
- Go to **Settings** > **Topology** > **Decoy Connectors**.
-
Select the Decoy Connector you want to modify, and then click **Actions** > **Edit**.
[See image.](#action-edit-decoy-connector-option)
-
In the **Decoy Connector Details** window, modify the required fields.
[See image.](#action-edit-decoy-connector-details)
- Click **Save**.
Deleting a Decoy Connector
To delete a Decoy Connector:
- Go to **Settings** > **Topology** > **Decoy Connectors**.
-
Select the Decoy Connector you want to delete, and then click **Actions** > **Delete**.
[See image.](#action-delete-decoy-connector-option)
- In the confirmation window, click **OK**.
