# Editing or Deleting a Landmine Decoy Personality
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/deception/editing-or-deleting-landmine-decoy-personality
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1531535.pdf

You can edit or delete landmine decoy personalities that you configured in the Zscaler Deception Admin Portal.
Editing a Landmine Decoy Personality
To edit a landmine decoy personality:
- Go to **Miragemaker** > **Strategy Builder **> **Landmine**.
-
Under **Actions**, click the **Edit** icon for the decoy personality you want to modify.
[See image.](#zd-edit-option-ldp)
-
In the** **policy configuration window, modify the following configurations as required:
- [Policy](/deception/configuring-landmine-decoy-personality)
- [Selection Criterion](/deception/creating-landmine-policy)
- [Password Settings](/deception/configuring-password-settings)
- [Defense Evasion](/deception/configuring-defense-evasion)
- [Privilege Escalation](/deception/configuring-privilege-escalation)
- [Cloud Lures](/deception/configuring-cloud-lures)
- [Browser Lures](/deception/configuring-browser-lures)
- [Session Lures](/deception/configuring-session-lures)
- [File Decoys](/deception/configuring-file-decoys)
- [Lure Settings](/deception/configuring-lure-settings)
- [Advanced Deception Capabilities](/deception/enable-advanced-deception-capabilities)
[See image.](#zd-edit-window-ldp)
- Click **Submit**.
The default landmine decoy personalities cannot be edited.
Deleting a Landmine Decoy Personality
To delete a landmine decoy personality:
- Go to **Miragemaker** > **Strategy Builder **> **Landmine**.
-
Under **Actions**, click the **Delete** icon for the decoy personality you want to delete.
[See image.](#zd-delete-option-ldp)
- In the confirmation window, click **OK**.
The default landmine decoy personalities cannot be deleted.
