# Downloading a Threat Intelligence Decoy Personality
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/deception/downloading-threat-intelligence-decoy-personality
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1531529.pdf

You can download both default and custom Threat Intelligence (TI) decoy personalities individually as a JSON file.
To download a TI decoy personality:
- Go to **Miragemaker **> **Strategy Builder **> **Threat Intelligence Decoy**.
- Click the **Download **icon for the decoy personality that you want to download.
[See image.](#zd-download-tidp)
The TI decoy personality is downloaded to your system as a JSON file.
