# Viewing Disaster Recovery
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zsdk/viewing-disaster-recovery
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1520936.pdf

Disaster recovery ensures business continuity when Zscaler service is impacted globally. Disaster recovery is ideal for organizations that depend on the Zscaler cloud to remain operational during disastrous events by providing users with continued access to critical applications.
Disaster Recovery in the ZSDK Admin Portal
In the ZSDK Admin Portal, you can [enable disaster recovery](/zsdk/configuring-disaster-recovery) on the following:
- [Defined Application Segments](/zsdk/defining-and-managing-application-segments#edit)
- [App Connector Groups](/zsdk/managing-app-connector-groups#edit)
- [Private Service Edge Groups](/zsdk/managing-zsdk-private-service-edge-groups#edit)
When all have been enabled and deployed (i.e., App Connectors, Private Service Edges), you can configure the [disaster recovery settings](/zsdk/configuring-disaster-recovery#Settings).
Navigating Disaster Recovery
On the Disaster Recovery page (Configuring & Control > Administration Control > Disaster Recovery), you can go to the following:
- [Disaster Recovery Application Segments](#application)
- [Disaster Recovery App Connector Groups](#appconnector)
- [Disaster Recovery Private Service Edge Groups](#privateserviceedge)
- [Disaster Recovery Settings](/zsdk/configuring-disaster-recovery#Settings)
