# Zscaler External Attack Surface Management (EASM) Logs
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/logs-fair-use/easm-logs
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1504036.pdf

For Zscaler External Attack Surface Management ( EASM), logs are defined as public internet-facing assets that are discovered and scanned by Zscaler EASM to identify and analyze potential external attack surfaces. These public internet-facing corporate assets include:
- Domains
- Hosts
- Web pages
- SSL/TLS certificates
- IP addresses
Logs are stored in the United States. Zscaler will delete the logs upon request. When the subscription term ends or expires, the logs are deleted by Zscaler according to applicable retention cycles.
