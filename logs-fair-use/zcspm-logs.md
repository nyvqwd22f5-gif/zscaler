# ZCSPM Logs
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/logs-fair-use/zcspm-logs
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1392906.pdf

Cloud configuration data processed by Zscaler in connection with your organization's use of Zscaler Cloud Security Posture Management (ZCSPM) are retained by Zscaler for a rolling period of 90 days during the term of subscription. When the subscription term ends or expires, Zscaler deletes the logs after the 90 day retention cycle.
