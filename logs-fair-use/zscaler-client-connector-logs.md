# Zscaler Client Connector Logs
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/logs-fair-use/zscaler-client-connector-logs
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1391061.pdf

For Zscaler Client Connector,* logs* are packet capture logs of your transactions. You can globally enable or disable the packet capture. You have unlimited access to these logs and can delete them from a laptop, desktop, or personal mobile device.
