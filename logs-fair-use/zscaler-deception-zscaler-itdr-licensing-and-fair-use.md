# Zscaler Deception and Zscaler ITDR Licensing and Fair Use
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/logs-fair-use/zscaler-deception-zscaler-itdr-licensing-and-fair-use
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1409131.pdf

Zscaler Deception and Zscaler ITDR are Software as a Service (SaaS) products that are licensed according to the number of seats purchased for the subscription term in an order. A *seat* is a subscription license for the Deception and ITDR service, respectively, for an individual authorized user. The number of seats required is determined by counting the total number of unique individual users of Deception and ITDR service over the previous rolling 90-day period.
Ensure that you order the correct number of seats for both Zscaler Deception and Zscaler ITDR. If you require additional seats during the subscription term, contact Zscaler Sales or your Channel Partner. Zscaler periodically reviews the number of seats to ensure license compliance.
The usages of Deception and ITDR are expected to be in accordance with the [Acceptable Use Policy](/legal/acceptable-use-policy).
