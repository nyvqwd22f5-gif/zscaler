# Zscaler Business Insights Logs
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/logs-fair-use/zscaler-business-insights-logs
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1443666.pdf

By accessing the Zscaler Business Insights service, you provide Zscaler the right to process, use, reproduce, store, modify, and display the information from logs, as defined in this article. For Zscaler Business Insights,* logs* are defined as follows:
- SaaS Application Usage Logs - Zscaler Business Insights generates logs via Zscaler Internet Access (ZIA) logs to create usage statistics for SaaS providers. Zscaler Business Insights also generates logs via Zscaler Business Insights connectors that you have configured` to collect usage data from SaaS providers.
- Workplace Insights Logs - Zscaler Business Insights generates logs via ZIA logs and via Zscaler Business Insights connectors that you have configured to collect data from SaaS providers to create insights.
Zscaler retains SaaS Application Usage Logs and Workplace Insights Logs for a rolling period of up to three years during your subscription term. When the subscription term ends or expires, the logs are deleted by Zscaler according to the applicable retention cycles.
SaaS Application Usage Logs and the Workplace Insights Logs are stored in the European Union.
