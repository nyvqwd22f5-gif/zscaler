# Zscaler Asset Exposure Management (AEM) Logs
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/logs-fair-use/zscaler-asset-exposure-management-aem-logs
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1520041.pdf

In order to provide the Zscaler Asset Exposure Management (AEM) service, Zscaler has the right to process, use, reproduce, store, modify, and display the information from logs. For AEM, *logs* are defined as the configuration and transaction data collected for actions performed by users authorized by you who are acting within the AEM SaaS Application.
Zscaler retains the logs on a rolling period up to a year during your subscription term. When the subscription term ends or expires, the logs are deleted by Zscaler according to applicable retention cycles.
Logs are stored in the United States.
