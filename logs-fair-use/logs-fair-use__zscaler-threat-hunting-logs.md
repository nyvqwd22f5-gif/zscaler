# Zscaler Threat Hunting Logs
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/logs-fair-use/zscaler-threat-hunting-logs
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1518281.pdf

In order to provide the Zscaler Threat Hunting service, Zscaler has the right to process, use, reproduce, store, modify, and display the information from logs as defined in this article. For Zscaler Threat Hunting, logs are defined as duplicates of applicable Zscaler SaaS products' logs.
Logs necessary to provide the Zscaler Threat Hunting service are processed and retained in the United States. After the analytical processes are complete, the results are transferred to, processed, and retained in the region of your choice.
Zscaler Threat Hunting retains the logs for a rolling period of 90 days. When the subscription term ends or expires, the logs are deleted by Zscaler according to applicable retention cycles.
