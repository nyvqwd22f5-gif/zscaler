# End-of-Sale & End-of-Life Notice for Zscaler Shift
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/eos-eol/eos-eol-notice-zscaler-shift
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1408171.pdf

Zscaler is announcing the End-of-Sale (EOS) and End-of-Life (EOL) dates for Zscaler Shift (also known as Zscaler Intelligent Routing for Guest Wi-Fi Protection). Shift is an easy-to-use cloud service that provides carrier-grade security and compliance for your organization's network.
| EOS Date | EOL Date |
| -------- | -------- |
| November 1, 2022 | November 1, 2024 |
As of November 1, 2022, Intelligent Routing Guest Wi-Fi SKUs are no longer available for purchase. Existing contracts will not be renewed beyond the EOL date. Existing deployments of Shift will be supported until the EOL date.
