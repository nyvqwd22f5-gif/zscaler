# End-of-Sale & End-of-Life Notice for Zscaler Cloud Identity Entitlement Management
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/eos-eol/end-sale-and-end-life-notice-zscaler-cloud-identity-entitlement-management
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1409741.pdf

Zscaler is announcing the End-of-Sale (EOS) and End-of-Life (EOL) dates for Zscaler Cloud Identity Entitlement Management (CIEM).
| EOS Date | EOL Date |
| -------- | -------- |
| October 18, 2022 | October 18, 2023 |
As of October 18, 2022, licenses for new CIEM subscriptions will not be available for purchase. The CIEM service and all existing tenants will continue to be supported until the EOL date.
