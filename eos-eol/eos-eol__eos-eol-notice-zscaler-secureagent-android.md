# End-of-Sale & End-of-Life for Zscaler SecureAgent on Android
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/eos-eol/eos-eol-notice-zscaler-secureagent-android
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1327806.pdf

Zscaler is announcing the End-of-Sale (EOS) and End-of-Life dates for Zscaler SecureAgent for Android. This also includes deployment using advanced API-based integration with MDM partners, such as MobileIron and AirWatch.
| **EOS Date** | **EOL Date** |
| ------------ | ------------ |
| August 1, 2018 | March 1, 2019 |
As of August 1, 2018, user licenses for new deployments of Android devices with Zscaler SecureAgent for Android are not available for purchase.
Existing deployments of Android will be supported until EOL of Zscaler SecureAgent for Android on March 1, 2019.
Next Steps
If you are using SecureAgent on Android devices, you must plan a migration to Zscaler Client Connector for Android. You must migrate to Zscaler Client Connector on Android by March 1, 2019, as Zscaler SecureAgent for Android will be EOL and no longer supported.
Additional Information
If you have additional questions, contact Zscaler Support via the Support link in the Admin Portal.
