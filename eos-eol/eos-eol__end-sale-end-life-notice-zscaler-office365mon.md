# End-of-Sale & End-of-Life Notice for Zscaler Office365Mon
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/eos-eol/end-sale-end-life-notice-zscaler-office365mon
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1411341.pdf

Zscaler is announcing the End-of-Sale (EOS) and End-of-Life (EOL) dates for Zscaler Office365Mon (Office 365 Monitoring). To learn more, see [https://www.office365mon.com/](https://www.office365mon.com/).
| EOS Date | EOL Date |
| -------- | -------- |
| October 20, 2022 | October 20, 2023 |
As of October 20, 2022, subscriptions for Office365Mon will not be available for purchase. The Office365Mon service and all existing tenants will continue to be supported until the EOL date.
