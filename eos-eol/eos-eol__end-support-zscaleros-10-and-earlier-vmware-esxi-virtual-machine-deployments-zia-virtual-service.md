# End-of-Support for ZscalerOS 10 and earlier on VMware ESXi Virtual Machine Deployments of Index Tool, NSS, ZAB, ZIA Virtual Service Edge, VZEN, and Zscaler Incident Receiver
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/eos-eol/end-support-zscaleros-10-and-earlier-vmware-esxi-virtual-machine-deployments-zia-virtual-service
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1395426.pdf

VMware ESXi Virtual Machine deployments with ZscalerOS version 10 and earlier are no longer supported for Index Tool, NSS, ZAB, ZIA Virtual Service Edge, VZEN, and Zscaler Incident Receiver as of June 24, 2022.
If your organization is running an ESXi Virtual Machine deployment with ZscalerOS version 10 and earlier, migrate to ZscalerOS version 24 or higher by June 24, 2022, to ensure service continuity.
Announcement date: May 4, 2022
