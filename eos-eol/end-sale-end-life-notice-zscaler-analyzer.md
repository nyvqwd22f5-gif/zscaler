# End-of-Sale & End-of-Life Notice for Zscaler Analyzer
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/eos-eol/end-sale-end-life-notice-zscaler-analyzer
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1471996.pdf

Zscaler is announcing the End-of-Sale (EOS) and End-of-Life (EOL) dates for Zscaler Analyzer.
| EOS Date | EOL Date |
| -------- | -------- |
| February 28, 2024 | February 28, 2025 |
As of February 28, 2024, new downloads for the Zscaler Analyzer tool and functionality of the related zmtr.zscaler.com website will not be available. Existing deployments of Zscaler Analyzer will be supported until EOL on February 28, 2025.
Next Steps
If you are currently using Zscaler Analyzer, you must plan a migration to Zscaler Digital Experience (ZDX).
Announcement date: December 5, 2023
