# End-of-Support for SpiderMonkey-Based PAC Parser
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/eos-eol/end-support-spidermonkey-based-pac-parser
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1458636.pdf

On January 31, 2024, Zscaler will no longer support the SpiderMonkey-based PAC parsers. All customers will be moved to the V8 PAC parser. Zscaler recommends customers test and migrate to V8 before the deprecation date.
Announcement date: July 21, 2023
