# Managing Firewall Policies
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zero-trust-branch/managing-firewall-policies
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1509381.pdf

You can view, edit, clone, delete, or organize firewall policies at global, template, or site levels. The** **system (default) policies** **created for each site are editable but cannot be reordered.
Editing a Policy
To edit a policy:
- Go to **Firewall **> **Policies**. Click the tab for the scope (**Global Policies**, **Template Policies**, or **Site Policies**) that includes the policy you want to edit.
-
Locate the policy that you want to edit, click the corresponding **Gear **icon, and select **Edit**.
[See image.](#edit-button-policy)
-
In the **Edit Policy **drawer, make the necessary changes.
[See image.](#edit-drawer-policy)
-
Click **Save**.
The policy changes are saved.
-
Click **Commit **to apply the changes.
[See image.](#commit-after-edit)
Reordering Policies
You can reorder policies to change the order of evaluation.
To reorder policies:
- Go to **Firewall **> **Policies**. Click the tab for the scope (**Global Policies**, **Template Policies**, or **Site Policies**) under which you want to reorder policies.
-
Click **Reorder**.
[See image.](#reorder-button)
A **Drag **icon appears for each custom policy.
-
Use the **Drag **icon to rearrange the policies as required. You can either click and drag the policies using the **Drag **icon or click the **Drag **icon to select the policy name after which the policy must be inserted.
[See image.](#reordering-policies)
-
Click **Save Policy Order**.
[See image.](#save-after-reorder)
Cloning a Policy
You can clone an existing policy and create a new policy with minimal changes.
To clone a policy:
- Go to **Firewall **> **Policies**. Click the tab for the scope (**Global Policies**, **Template Policies**, or **Site Policies**) under which you want to clone a policy.
-
Locate the policy that you want to clone, click the corresponding **Gear **icon, and select **Clone**.
[See image.](#clone-button)
-
In the **Clone <policy_name> Policy **drawer, make the necessary changes.
[See image.](#clone-drawer)
-
Click **Clone**.
The new policy is added.
-
Click **Commit **to apply the changes.
[See image.](#commit-after-clone)
Deleting a Policy
To delete a policy:
- Go to **Firewall **> **Policies**. Click the tab for the scope (**Global Policies**, **Template Policies**, or **Site Policies**) that includes the policy you want to delete.
-
Locate the policy that you want to delete, click the corresponding **Gear **icon, and select **Delete**.
[See image.](#delete-button)
-
In the delete confirmation window, enter `DELETE` and click **Confirm**.
[See image.](#delete-confirmation)
-
Click **Commit **to apply the changes.
[See image.](#commit-after-delete)
The policy is removed from the Zero Trust Branch Admin Portal.
