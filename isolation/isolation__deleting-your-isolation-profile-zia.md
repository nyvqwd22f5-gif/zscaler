# Deleting Your Isolation Profile for ZIA
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/isolation/deleting-your-isolation-profile-zia
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1447281.pdf

After [creating a ZIA isolation profile](/isolation/creating-zia-isolation-profile-isolation), you can [edit](/isolation/editing-your-zia-isolation-profile-0) or delete the profile at any time.
[Default isolation profiles](/isolation/default-isolation-profiles-isolation) cannot be deleted.
To delete your ZIA isolation profile:
- Go to **Administration** > **Secure Browsing** > **Browser** **Isolation**.
The **Isolation Profiles** page appears.
[See image.](#Admin_Secure-Browsing_Browser-Isolation)
- Click the **Edit** icon for the chosen profile.
The **Edit Isolation Profile** window appears.
[See image.](#Iso-Profiles_Edit-button)
- Click **Delete**.
[See image.](#Edit-Iso-Profile_Delete-button)
- The **Delete Isolation Profile** window appears and asks you to confirm. Click **Delete**.
[See image.](#Delete-isolation-profile-confirm-window)
- The page refreshes, and a temporary message appears to confirm the isolation profile has been deleted.
[See image.](#Profiles-List_Item-Deleted)
