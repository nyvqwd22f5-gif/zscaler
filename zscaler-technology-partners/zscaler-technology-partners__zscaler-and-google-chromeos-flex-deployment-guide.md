# Zscaler and Google ChromeOS Flex Deployment Guide
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zscaler-technology-partners/zscaler-and-google-chromeos-flex-deployment-guide
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1531230.pdf

The Zscaler and Google ChromeOS Flex Deployment Guide provides instructions on integrating Zscaler Internet Access (ZIA) and Zscaler Private Access (ZPA) with Google ChromeOS Flex.
Google ChromeOS Flex is a cloud-first, fast, and secure operating system that allows you to refresh your existing PCs and Macs by installing ChromeOS on them. It's a free, lightweight alternative to traditional operating systems like Windows or macOS, designed for users who primarily rely on web-based applications and services.
