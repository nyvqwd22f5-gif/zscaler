# Zscaler and Aruba EdgeConnect (Silver Peak) Deployment Guide
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zscaler-technology-partners/zscaler-and-aruba-edgeconnect-silver-peak-deployment-guide
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1390506.pdf

The Zscaler and Aruba EdgeConnect (Silver Peak) Deployment Guide provides instructions on how to configure Zscaler Internet Access (ZIA) to work with Aruba (Silver Peak) SD-WAN.
The Aruba EdgeConnect unified SD-WAN platform enables customers to build a unified WAN edge that is business-driven, delivers the highest quality of experience, continuously adapts to changing business needs and network conditions. ZIA and EdgeConnect integrate and work together under the following scenarios:
- Creating Automated and Manual IPSec Tunnels
- Creating Sub-Locations and Gateway Options
- Enabling Layer-7 Health Checks for Automated Tunnels
For more information on Aruba as a Zscaler partner, see the [Aruba partner page](https://www.zscaler.com/partners/aruba).
