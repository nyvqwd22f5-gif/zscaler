The Zscaler UVM and Bitsight Deployment Guide provides instructions on using Zscaler Unified Vulnerability Management (UVM) features with the Bitsight Security Ratings product.
Bitsight Security Ratings is a cyber risk quantification and monitoring platform designed to provide continuous, data-driven visibility into an organizationâ€™s external security posture.
Zscaler's UVM solution ingests, normalizes, and unifies data across enterprise security and business systems to deliver actionable insights, analytics, and operational efficiencies. Zscaler UVM offers the following preconfigured Bitsight connectors:
- Bitsight Findings: Retrieves information about findings, which are security-related events or configurations on an asset, such as a vulnerability, detection of a botnet, or a port detected to be open.
- Bitsight Observations: Retrieve detailed information (observations) about the risk category data of companies in your portfolio.
