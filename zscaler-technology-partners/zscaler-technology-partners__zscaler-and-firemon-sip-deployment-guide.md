# Zscaler and FireMon Deployment Guide
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zscaler-technology-partners/zscaler-and-firemon-sip-deployment-guide
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1390121.pdf

The Zscaler and FireMon Deployment Guide provides instructions on integrating Zscaler Internet Access (ZIA) and FireMon Security Intelligence Platform (SIP).
The FireMon SIP helps organizations gain visibility into and control over their complex network security infrastructures.
