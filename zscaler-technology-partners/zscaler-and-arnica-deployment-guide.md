# Zscaler and Arnica Deployment Guide
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zscaler-technology-partners/zscaler-and-arnica-deployment-guide
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1528925.pdf

The Zscaler UVM and Arnica Deployment Guide provides instructions on using Zscaler Unified Vulnerability Management (UVM) with the Arnica Security platform.
The Zscaler UVM solution ingests, normalizes, and unifies data across enterprise security and business systems to deliver actionable insights, analytics, and operational efficiencies. Zscaler UVM offers an Arnica Miconfigs preconfigured Arnica connector, which retrieves misconfigurations in your projects.
