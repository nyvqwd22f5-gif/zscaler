# Zscaler UVM and Apiiro Deployment Guide
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zscaler-technology-partners/zscaler-uvm-and-apiiro-deployment-guide
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1529119.pdf

The Zscaler UVM and Apiiro Deployment Guide provides instructions on using Zscaler Unified Vulnerability Management (UVM) features with the Apiiro platform.
The Zscaler UVM solution ingests, normalizes, and unifies data across enterprise security and business systems to deliver actionable insights, analytics, and operational efficiencies.
Zscaler UVM offers a preconfigured Apiiro connectors for Apiiro Secrets, which focuses on managing and securing access to sensitive information within applications.
