# Zscaler UVM and Horizon3.ai Deployment Guide
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zscaler-technology-partners/zscaler-uvm-and-horizon3-ai-deployment-guide
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1529397.pdf

The Zscaler UVM and Horizon3.ai Deployment Guide provides instructions on using Zscaler Unified Vulnerability Management (UVM) features with the Horizon3.ai NodeZero platform.
Zscaler's UVM solution ingests, normalizes, and unifies data across enterprise security and business systems to deliver actionable insights, analytics, and operational efficiencies.
Zscaler UVM offers the following pre-configured Horizon3 NodeZero connectors:
- Horizon3 Assets: Retrieves information about the discovered assets, including their attributes and states.
- Horizon3 Flat Impacts: Retrieves impacts of weaknesses exploited within your environment.
- Horizon3 Weaknesses Evidence: Retrieves proof of vulnerabilities.
