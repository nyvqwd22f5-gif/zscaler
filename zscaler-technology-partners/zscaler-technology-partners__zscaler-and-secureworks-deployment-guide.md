# Zscaler and Secureworks Deployment Guide
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zscaler-technology-partners/zscaler-and-secureworks-deployment-guide
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1457671.pdf

The Zscaler and Secureworks Deployment Guide provides instructions on how to integrate Zscaler Internet Access (ZIA) to work with Secureworks Taegis XDR Data Collector.
Secureworks Taegis is the cybersecurity analytics cloud platform built on real-world threat intelligence and research. Taegis detects the most advanced threats, streamlines collaboration between your team and ours, and automatically spotlights the most important actions first.
Zscaler is configured to send logs via Nanolog Streaming Service (NSS) and Cloud NSS to a Taegis XDR Data Collector.
