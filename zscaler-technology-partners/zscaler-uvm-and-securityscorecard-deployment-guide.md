# Zscaler UVM and SecurityScorecard Deployment Guide
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zscaler-technology-partners/zscaler-uvm-and-securityscorecard-deployment-guide
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1528913.pdf

The Zscaler UVM and SecurityScorecard Deployment Guide provides instructions on using Zscaler Unified Vulnerability Management (UVM) with the SecurityScorecard platform.
Zscaler UVM ingests, normalizes, and unifies data across enterprise security and business systems to deliver actionable insights, analytics, and operational efficiencies.
Zscaler UVM offers the following preconfigured SecurityScorecard connectors:
- SecurityScorecard: Retreives information about security issues or vulnerabilities detected within an organization's systems, networks, or applications.
- SecurityScorecard Alerts: Retrieves notifications or alerts about potential security incidents or suspicious activities received in your SecurityScorecard account.
