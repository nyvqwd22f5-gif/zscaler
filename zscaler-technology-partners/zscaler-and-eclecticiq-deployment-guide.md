# Zscaler and EclecticIQ Deployment Guide
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zscaler-technology-partners/zscaler-and-eclecticiq-deployment-guide
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1403271.pdf

The Zscaler and EclecticIQ Deployment Guide provides instructions on how to integrate Zscaler Internet Access (ZIA) features with the EclecticIQ platform.
The EclecticIQ platform delivers intelligence to the network and provides threat hunting automation and collaboration, forensic-depth endpoint visibility, and threat detection and response. Zscaler integrates with EclecticIQ by consuming high-confidence indicators to enforce real-time policies and ensure all users are completely protected from emerging threats and targeted attacks.
