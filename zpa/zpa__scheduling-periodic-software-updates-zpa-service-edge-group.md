# Scheduling Periodic Software Updates for a ZPA Private Service Edge Group
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zpa/scheduling-periodic-software-updates-zpa-service-edge-group
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1484521.pdf

To schedule a periodic software update for a ZPA Private Service Edge group:
- Go to **Configuration & Control** > **Private Infrastructure** > **Private Service Edge Management** > **Private Service Edges**.
- Click the **Private Service Edge Groups** tab.
- In the table, locate the ZPA Private Service Edge group and click the **Edit** icon.
- In the **Edit Private Service Edge Group** window, under **Private Service Edge Software Update Schedule**, choose the day of the week and start time for the periodic update.
![Modifying the Private Service Edge Software Update Schedule for a Private Service Edge group within ZPA](/downloads/zpa/private-service-edge-management/private-service-edge-software-updates/scheduling-periodic-software-updates-zpa-service-edge-group/Edit%20PSE%20Group%20window.png)
- Click **Save**.
To confirm that the update was scheduled, go to the [Private Service Edge Groups](/zpa/about-zpa-service-edge-groups) page and verify that the proper day and time is displayed in the **Next Periodic Software Update** column.
