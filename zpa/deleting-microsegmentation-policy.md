# Deleting a Microsegmentation Policy
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zpa/deleting-microsegmentation-policy
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1531969.pdf

You can [configure](/zpa/configuring-microsegmentation-policies), [edit](/zpa/editing-microsegmentation-policy), and delete policy rules as necessary. Any configurations, edits, or deletions of policy rules take effect immediately.
To delete a Microsegmentation policy rule:
- Go to **Microsegmentation** > **Policy**.
- Click the **Resource Policy** tab.
- Click the **Delete **icon.
[See image.](#Edit-icon)
- A confirmation window appears. Click **Delete**.
[See image.](#Delete-confirmation-window)
