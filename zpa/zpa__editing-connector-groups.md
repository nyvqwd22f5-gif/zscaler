# Editing App Connector Groups
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zpa/editing-connector-groups
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1483596.pdf

This article describes how to edit an App Connector group. To learn about configuring an App Connector group and the available options, see [About App Connector Groups](/zpa/about-connector-groups) or [Configuring a Version Profile](/zpa/configuring-version-profile).
To edit a configured App Connector group:
- Go to **Configuration & Control** > **Private Infrastructure** > **App Connector Management** > **App Connector Groups**.
-
In the table, locate the App Connector group you want to modify and click the **Edit **icon.
App Connector groups that are managed by Zscaler are read only and cannot be edited.
The **Edit App Connector Group** window appears.
- In the **Edit App Connector Group** window, you can modify any field.
![Edit App Connector Group window within the ZPA Admin Portal](/downloads/zpa/app-connector-management/app-connector-groups/editing-connector-groups/Updatededitedappconngroup_0.png)
- Click **Save**.
