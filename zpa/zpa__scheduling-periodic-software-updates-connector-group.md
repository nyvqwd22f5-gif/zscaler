# Scheduling Periodic Software Updates for an App Connector Group
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zpa/scheduling-periodic-software-updates-connector-group
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1483851.pdf

To schedule a periodic software update for an App Connector group:
- Go to **Configuration & Control** > **Private Infrastructure** > **App Connector Management** > **App Connector Groups**.
- In the table, locate the App Connector group and click the **Edit **icon.
- In the **Edit App Connector Group** window, under **App Connector Software Update Schedule**, choose the day of the week and start time for the periodic update.
![Edit App Connector Group window within the ZPA Admin Portal](/downloads/zpa/documentation-knowledgebase/connectors/about-connector-periodic-software-update/how-do-i-schedule-periodic-software-update-connector-group/zpa-editconnectorgroup-updateschedule.png)
- Click **Save**.
To confirm that the update was scheduled, go to the [App Connector Groups](/zpa/about-connectorgroups) page and verify that the proper day and time is displayed in the **Next Periodic Software Update** column.
