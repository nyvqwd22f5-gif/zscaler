# Editing Privileged Credentials
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zpa/editing-privileged-credentials
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1485556.pdf

To edit a privileged credential:
- Go to **Administration** > **Privileged Remote Access** > **Privileged Credentials**.
- In the table, locate the privileged credential you want to modify and click the **Edit** icon.
The **Edit Privileged Credential** window appears.
