# Editing Microtenants
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zpa/editing-microtenants
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1485691.pdf

To edit a Microtenant:
- Go to **Configuration & Control **>** Administration Control **> **Microtenants**.
- In the table, locate the Microtenant you want to modify and click the **Edit **icon.
The **Edit Microtenant** drawer appears.
-
In the **Edit Microtenant** drawer, you can modify any field. To learn more, see [Configuring Microtenants](/zpa/configuring-microtenants).
![Edit Microtenant drawer ](/downloads/zpa/administration/delegated-tenant-administration/editing-microtenants/zpa-edit-microtenant-drawer.png)
- Click **Save**.
