# Deleting Backups
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zpa/deleting-backups
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1485861.pdf

To delete a [backup](/zpa/about-backup-and-restore):
- Go to **Configuration & Control** > **Administration Control** > **Backup and Restore**.
-
Locate the **Backup Name** within the table and click the **Delete **(![zpa-delete-icon.png](/downloads/zpa/administration/backup-restore/deleting-restore-points/zpa-delete-icon.png)
) icon.
The **Delete Backup **window appears.
![Delete Backup window](/downloads/zpa/administration/backup-restore/deleting-restore-points/zpa-delete-backup.png)
- Click **Delete**.
Deleting a backup also deletes the linked [restore activities](/zpa/viewing-restore-activities-and-restore-reports). For example, after deleting a backup, information about who restored the backup and the restore reports are deleted.
