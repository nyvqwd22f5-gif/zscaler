# Editing AppZones
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zpa/editing-appzones
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1531943.pdf

Admins can [configure](/zpa/configuring-appzones), edit, and [delete](/zpa/deleting-appzones) [AppZones](/zpa/about-appzone-page) as needed.
To edit an AppZone:
- Go to **Microsegmentation** > **Resource Management**.
- Click the **AppZone** tab.
[See image.](#Resource-Management_AppZone_Edit-AppZone)
- Click the **Edit** icon for the AppZone you want to edit.
- In the **Edit AppZone **window, modify the information as necessary. To learn more, see [Configuring AppZones](/zpa/configuring-appzones).
[See image.](#Edit_AppZone_window)
- Click **Save**.
Your updated AppZone appears in the list of AppZones.
