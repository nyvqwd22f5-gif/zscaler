# Editing or Deleting Items
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zia/editing-deleting-items
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1402176.pdf

You can edit or delete the configured items in your Zscaler Partner Portal account. For example, you can edit or delete the configured users, or policy templates.
Editing Items
To edit items:
- Go to the item you want to edit.
- Click the **Edit** icon, as shown in the following example.
![Edit in Management Portal](/downloads/tech-pubs-drafts/zia-draft-articles/editing-or-deleting-items/Edit%20Admins_0.png)
- Make the changes and click **Save **or **Save & Sync**.
Deleting Items
To delete items with the Edit icon:
- Go to the item you want to delete.
- Click the** Edit **icon.
-
Click **Delete & Sync**.
[See image.](#delete-sync)
To delete items with the Delete icon:
- Go to the item you want to delete.
-
Click the **Delete **icon.
![Delete icon](/downloads/tech-pubs-drafts/miscellaneous-draft-articles/editing-or-deleting-items/Delete%20icon1.png)
-
Read the warning message that appears. The following is an example.
[See image.](#confirm-delete)
- Click **Confirm **to delete the item.
[]![Delete & Sync window](/downloads/tech-pubs-drafts/zia-draft-articles/editing-or-deleting-items/Delete%20and%20sync.png)
[]![Delete Confirmation Window](/downloads/tech-pubs-drafts/miscellaneous-draft-articles/editing-or-deleting-items/Delete%20icon.png)
