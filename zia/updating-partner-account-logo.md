# Updating Partner Account Logo
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zia/updating-partner-account-logo
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1402046.pdf

You can update your account logo or set the background color in the Zscaler Partner Portal. Your partner account logo is used for white labeling your tenant's ZIA Admin Portal if you've enabled [white labeling](/zia/modifying-your-account-settings).
To update your account:
- [Upload an image for the logo](#image)
- [Set the background color](#color)
[]To update your account logo with an image:
- Go to **Settings** page.
-
Click **Upload Logo**.
[See image.](#logo-image)
- Upload the logo from your local directory. The service supports a logo with the following features:
- Dimensions of 300 x 220 pixels or less.
- Size of 10 KB or less.
- .png, .jpg, or .gif file types.
- Click **Save**.
[]To update the background color:
- Go to **Settings** page.
- Click **Change Background Color**.
-
Select a color from the list or enter the hexadecimal code of the color you want to update.
[See image.](#color-image)
- Click **Save**.
