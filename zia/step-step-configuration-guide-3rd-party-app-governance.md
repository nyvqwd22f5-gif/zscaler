# Step-by-Step Configuration Guide for 3rd-Party App Governance
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zia/step-step-configuration-guide-3rd-party-app-governance
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1463516.pdf

This guide takes you through the configuration steps to begin using Zscaler 3rd-Party App Governance for your organization.
Before you begin configuring 3rd-Party App Governance, Zscaler recommends reading [What Is 3rd-Party App Governance?](/zia/what-3rd-party-app-governance)
Prerequisites
You must be authorized to access the 3rd-Party App Governance Admin Portal. Contact your Zscaler Account team for more information.
