# About Zscaler Analyzer
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zia/about-zscaler-analyzer
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1398961.pdf

To verify connection speed issues, you can use the Zscaler Analyzer app to perform an MTR (My Traceroute) and send the results to Zscaler Support. This test checks the quality of the connection between your device and the ZIA Public Service Edge. For a more in-depth look at Zscaler Analyzer and its features, go to [https://zmtr.zscaler.com/](https://zmtr.zscaler.com/).
To download Zscaler Analyzer:
- Go to [https://zmtr.zscaler.com/](https://zmtr.zscaler.com/).
- Download the Zscaler Analyzer for your operating system. You can download Zscaler Analyzer for **Windows** or **Linux**.
[See image.](#Image1)
[![Screenshot of zmtr.zscaler.com highlighting the Windows, Mac OS X, and Linux (Beta) options you can choose to download the Zscaler Analyzer](/downloads/zia/troubleshooting/about-zscaler-analyzer/ZIA_zscaler_analyzer_download_page_screenshot.png)
]
After you've installed Zscaler Analyzer, open the app. Log in to the Zscaler service, if necessary.
To perform an MTR:
- Click **Start Test **to perform the MTR and retrieve your IP address and ZIA Public Service Edge information.
[See image.](#Image2)
- Once the MTR is complete, click **Export All Results**. The results are saved to a CSV file.
[See image.](#Image3)
- Attach the CSV file to a Zscaler Support ticket and submit it.
