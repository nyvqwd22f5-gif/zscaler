# About Role Management
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zscaler-client-connector/about-role-management
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1414411.pdf

You can create roles to control access to Zscaler Client Connector Portal settings and assign these roles to Zscaler Internet Access (ZIA), Zscaler Private Access (ZPA), and Zscaler Digital Experience (ZDX) administrators.
About the Role Management Page
On the Role Management page (Administration > Administration Management), you can:
- [Add a role](/zscaler-client-connector/adding-roles).
- View the following information for roles:
- **Role Name**: Name of role created by the super admin.
- **Full Access**: Settings that the role has access to configure.
- **View-Only Access**: Settings that the role can only view.
- [Edit a role](/zscaler-client-connector/assigning-administrator-custom-role).
- [Delete a role](/zscaler-client-connector/editing-or-deleting-items-zscaler-client-connector-portal).
- View a role.
- Go to the [Administrators page](/zscaler-client-connector/about-role-management-zscaler-client-connector).
