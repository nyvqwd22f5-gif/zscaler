# About Admins in the Management Portal for Partners
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zia/about-admins-management-portal-partners
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1402111.pdf

Zscaler partners can create admin users with different permissions to manage intended tenants or tenant groups on the Management Portal for Partners. You can see all the configured admin users on the Admins page.
The Admins page provides the following benefits and enables you to:
- View a complete list of admins on your Zscaler partner account.
- Create a new admin.
- Assign or modify an admin's permissions.
- Assign or revoke management of tenants or tenant groups to an admin.
About the Admins Page
On the Admins page, you can do the following:
- View a list of configured admins. For each admin, you can see:
- **Admin Name**: The name of the admin user.
- **Login ID**: A unique login ID assigned to the admin as per their organization's domain.
- **Email ID**: The admin's email ID.
- **Role**: The role assigned to the admin user.
- **Number of Tenants**: The number of tenants assigned to the admin.
- **Comments**: Comments about the admin, if any.
- **Actions**: The options to manage admins (i.e., preview, edit, and delete).
