# Exporting Your Client IDs from Google Workspace
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zia/exporting-your-client-ids-google-workspace
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1450371.pdf

You can export a list of client IDs of all the apps connected to your Google environment and [upload](/zia/uploading-apps-bulk) the apps to the Inventory** **in Zscaler 3rd-Party App Governance** **using the client IDs.
Google Workspace Admin Console
Google Workspace admins can navigate to [App Access Control](https://admin.google.com/u/1/ac/owl/list?tab=apps) and find all the third-party apps connected to their Google environment.
Google Account Security
Any Google user can navigate to [account security settings and information](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions) and find the apps with access to their account. There you can find the list of apps with access to your Google account data and apps using your Google account for sign-in. You can copy the app name and search for it in the** Inventory**.
Gmail Activity Information
In your Gmail inbox, you can find the apps that have accessed your inbox. Go to your Gmail inbox, scroll to the bottom of the page, and click the **Details** button.
