# Adding a Zscaler Authentication Bridge
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zia/adding-zscaler-authentication-bridge
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1400016.pdf

Configuring a Zscaler Authentication Bridge (ZAB) is one of the tasks you must complete when deploying a ZAB. For a complete list of tasks, see [Deploying a Zscaler Authentication Bridge](/zia/how-do-i-deploy-zscaler-authentication-bridge).
[Watch a video about the Zscaler Authentication Bridge.](https://fast.wistia.net/embed/iframe/ulvay54489)
To add a ZAB:
- Go to **Administration**** **>** Authentication Settings**.
- Click the** Authentication Bridges **tab.
- Click **Add Authentication Bridge**.
The **Add Authentication Bridge** window appears.
- In the **Add Authentication Bridge** window:
- **Name**: Enter a name for the ZAB.
- **Status**:** Enable** or **Disable** the ZAB. The ZAB is enabled by default.
- Click **Save** and [activate the change](/zia/saving-and-activating-changes-admin-portal).
