# About Scheduled Reports
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zpc/about-scheduled-reports
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1457181.pdf

ZPC offers you to view or schedule reports for all available benchmarks.
The Scheduled Reports page provides the following benefits and enables you to:
- View all the scheduled compliance reports.
- Create a new compliance report.
On the Scheduled Reports page (Administration > Scheduled Reports), you can:
- [Schedule a compliance report.](/zpc/scheduling-compliance-reports)
- Search for a specific schedule report in the searchable columns.
- Modify the table and its columns. You can choose which columns appear on the report table. To learn more, see [Using Tables](/zpc/using-tables).
- [Edit or delete a schedule report.](/zpc/editing-or-deleting-scheduled-reports)
- [Enable or disable a schedule report.](/zpc/enabling-or-disabling-scheduled-reports)
