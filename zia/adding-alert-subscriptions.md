# Adding Alert Subscriptions
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zia/adding-alert-subscriptions
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1399166.pdf

[Watch a video about Alerts, including how to add an alert subscription](https://fast.wistia.net/embed/iframe/tuy0r93wba)
To subscribe and receive the alerts through email:
- Go to **Administration **>** ****Alerts**.
- Click the **Publish Alerts** tab.
- Click **Add Alert Subscription**.
The **Add Alert Subscription** window appears.
- In the **Add Alert Subscription** window:** **
- **Email**: Enter a valid email address.
- **Description**: (Optional) Enter any additional comments or information. The description cannot exceed 10,240 characters.
- For each [alert class](/zia/about-alerts), select the severity level of the alerts that the recipient receives.
- Click **Save** and [activate the change](/zia/saving-and-activating-changes-admin-portal).
