# Viewing Subscriptions
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zia/viewing-subscriptions
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1399806.pdf

[Watch a video about the Company Profile, including about Subscriptions](https://fast.wistia.net/embed/iframe/zu9gt2veq3)
Subscriptions define the various features and levels of functionality that are available to your organization. The **Subscriptions** tab lists the features subscribed to your account. To learn more, see [Zscaler Pricing and Plans](https://www.zscaler.com/pricing-and-plans).
Contact your Zscaler Sales Representative to update your subscriptions.
To view your subscriptions:
- Go to **Administration** > **Company Profile**.
- Click the **Subscriptions **tab.
[See image.](#subscriptionstab)
You can modify the table and its columns. To learn more, see [Using Tables](/zia/how-do-i-use-tables-admin-portal).
