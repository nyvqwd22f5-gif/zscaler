# Configuring the Attack Surface Report Notification
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zia/configuring-attack-surface-report-notification
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1401991.pdf

The [Attack Surface Report](/zia/attack-surface-report) notification keeps you posted on the latest available report for download by sending an email notification to the configured email address.
To configure an email notification:
- Go to **Analytics** > **Attack Surface Report**.
- Click **Notify**.
The **Notify** window appears.
- In the **Notify** window, enter the email address in the **Email Address** field, then click **Add Items**.
You can enter multiple entries. Hit **Enter** after each entry, then click **Add Items**. For item lists, you can view up to 32 items; filter the list by searching for a word, phrase, or number contained in an item; and remove all items from the list (**Remove All**) or only specific items from the list (**X**).
- Click **Submit**.
