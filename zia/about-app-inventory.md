# About the App Inventory
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zia/about-app-inventory
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1480641.pdf

The App Inventory enables you to gain the right visibility into, manage, and govern your third-party apps, add-ons, and extensions. You can automatically populate your inventory with apps by leveraging 3rd-Party App Governanceâ€™s discovery capabilities,[ connecting your various SaaS platforms to 3rd-Party App Governance](/zia/connecting-your-platforms-3rd-party-app-governance), manually [uploading apps](/zia/uploading-apps-bulk), and by [searching](/zia/searching-apps) and adding from the 3rd-Party App Governance catalog.
The App Inventory provides the following benefits and enables you to:
- Search and review a specific app, and filter apps by any given criteria (e.g., Permission Level, Risk Score, etc.).
- Pre-vet an app; change the app risk score; classify apps; take automated actions such as revoke, ban, or review; create custom views; generate reports;** **devise policies; and more.
About the App Inventory Page
On the App Inventory page, you can do the following:
- View a high-level overview of all the managed apps.
- Search for apps. To learn more, see [Searching for Apps](/zia/searching-apps). You can use the filter to view search results from the inventory, catalog, or both. By default, the search results from the inventory are displayed.
- Select a custom view. To learn more, see [Custom Views](/zia/custom-views).
- Set a selected custom view as favorite.
- Save a custom view.
- Set a policy for a custom view. To learn more, see [3rd-Party App Governance Policies](/zia/3rd-party-app-governance-policies).
- Apply filters to the apps in the inventory. To learn more, see [App Inventory Filters](/zia/app-inventory-filters).
- Export the apps report to a CSV file.
- Share a link of the list of apps in the inventory.
- Upload apps to the inventory. To learn more, see [Uploading Apps in Bulk](/zia/uploading-apps-bulk).
- View a list of all the apps in the inventory. For each app, you can view:
- **Status**: The status of the app.
- **App Name**: The name of the app. Click the app name to open the [App Panel](/zia/about-app-panel) and further review it.
- **Publisher**: The name of the app publisher.
- **Platform**: The platform on which the app is supported.
- **Origin**: The origin of the app. It can be one of the following:
- **1st Party**: The app is developed by the service provider integrated with 3rd-Party App Governance.
- **3rd Party**: The app is created by an external developer.
- **Internal App**: The app is developed for internal use within the organization.
- **Marketplace**: Indicates whether the app is publicly available in the marketplace.
- **Users**: The number of users using the app.
- **Usage**: The app usage details.
- **Risk Score**: The risk score for the app. You can change the risk score. To learn more, see [Updating the App Risk Score](/zia/updating-app-risk-score).
- **Access Type**: The type of application access.
- **Access Level**: The access level for the app.
- **Findings**: The number of app findings.
- **Classification**: The classification for the app. You can change the app's classification. To learn more, see [Classifying Apps](/zia/classifying-apps).
- **Automated Workflow**: The automated workflow for the app. You can change the app's automated workflow (e.g., [End User Review](/zia/configuring-end-user-review) or [Revoked / Banned](/zia/revoking-banning-apps)) if supported by the platform.
- Add the app to the inventory, if the app has yet to be added. To learn more, see [Add an App to Your Inventory](/zia/pre-vetting-apps#add-app-inventory).
