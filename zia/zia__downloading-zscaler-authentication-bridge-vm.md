# Downloading the Zscaler Authentication Bridge VM
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zia/downloading-zscaler-authentication-bridge-vm
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1401126.pdf

Downloading the Zscaler Authentication Bridge (ZAB) virtual machine (VM) is one of the tasks you must complete when deploying a ZAB. For a complete list of tasks, see [Deploying a Zscaler Authentication Bridge](/zia/how-do-i-deploy-zscaler-authentication-bridge).
To download the ZAB VM:
- Go to **Administration**** **>** Authentication Settings**.
- Click the **Authentication Bridges **tab.
- Click **Download ZB VM**.
The **Download ZAB VM **window appears.
- In the **Download ZAB VM **window, enter the **Number of Users** that the ZAB synchronizes, and optionally, authenticates.
- Click **Compute **to compute the appropriate resources for your ZAB. The recommended ZAB specifications appears. The ZAB can synchronize and authenticate hundreds of thousands of users. The ZAB specifications are determined by the number of users that the ZAB provisions.
[See image.](#seeimage1)
- Under** ZAB Virtual Machine**, click **Download ZAB VM **to download the ZAB VM.
- Click **Close**.
