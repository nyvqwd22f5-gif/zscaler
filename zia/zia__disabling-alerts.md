# Disabling Alerts
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zia/disabling-alerts
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1400366.pdf

You can disable alerts after creating them. When you disable an alert, ZIA stops sending alert notifications. To learn more, see [About Alerts](/zia/about-alerts).
[]To disable alerts:
-
Go to **Administration **>** Alerts**.
[See image.](#administration-image)
-
Click the** Edit** icon next to the alert you want to disable.
The **Edit Alert Definition** window appears.
[See image.](#edit-image)
-
In the **Edit Alert Definition** window, under **Status**, select **Disabled** from the drop-down menu.
[See image.](#disabled-image)
- Click **Save** and [activate the change](/zia/saving-and-activating-changes-admin-portal).
