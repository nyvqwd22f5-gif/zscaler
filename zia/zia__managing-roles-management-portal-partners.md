# Managing Roles in the Management Portal for Partners
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zia/managing-roles-management-portal-partners
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1506476.pdf

The Management Portal for Partners allows you to create your own customized [admin role templates](/zia/about-roles-in-the-management-portal-for-partners). You can further edit or delete existing role templates in your Zscaler partner account.
Adding a New Role
To create a new role template:
- Go to **Roles**.
- Click on the **Add Role** button. The **Add Role **window appears.
-
In the **Add Role** window, enter the following details:
- **Role Name**: The name of the role.
- **Permissions**: Define permissions for different sections.
- **Description**: Add a description for the role.
[See image.](#adding-roles)
- Click **Save**.

[]Editing Roles
To edit a role template:
- Go to **Roles**.
- Click the **Edit** icon. The **Edit Role** window appears.
-
Modify the permissions.
[See image.](#editing-roles-image)
- Click **Save**.

![Add Edit window](/downloads/zia/zscaler-management-portal-partners/administration/role-management/managing-roles-management-portal-partners/editing-roles.png)

Confirm your decision.
