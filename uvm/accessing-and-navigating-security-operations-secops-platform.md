# Accessing and Navigating the Zscaler Security Operations (SecOps) Platform
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/uvm/accessing-and-navigating-security-operations-secops-platform
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1531068.pdf

This article covers the following topics to help you get started with the Zscaler Security Operations (SecOps) platform:
- [Signing In to the SecOps Platform](#signing-in-secops-platform)
- [Accessing the Admin Settings](#accessing-admin-settings)
- [Navigating within the SecOps Platform](#navigating-secops-platform)
[]After your organization is provisioned for the SecOps platform, you receive an email with a username (this is your registered email address) and a password to sign in to the SecOps platform. Go to the login page provided in the email (app.avalor.io).
If your account is configured without single sign-on (SSO):
- Enter your email address.
-
Click **Continue**.
The **Login **page appears.
- Enter your email address and password.
- Click **Sign In**.
If your account is configured to use SSO:
- Enter your email address.
-
Click **Continue**.
You are redirected to your IdP's login page.
- Log in with your credentials. If you are logged in successfully, you are redirected to the SecOps platform.
[]The SecOps account admin can configure SSO, user management, and email notifications for data processing failures in the account.
To access the Account Settings:
- Click the **Profile **menu in the top-right corner of the page.
-
Click **Account Settings**.
[See image.](#secops-platform-account-settings)
- In the left-side navigation:
- Click **Settings **to configure and manage SSO (e.g., Okta).
- Click **User Management **to configure and manage [users](/uvm/creating-managing-users).
- Click **Permissions **to configure and manage [roles permissions](/uvm/managing-system-and-custom-roles), [content permissions](/uvm/creating-managing-content-permissions) and [email notifications](/uvm/enabling-email-notifications-source-run-failures).
[]![secops platform account settings ](/downloads/uvm/getting-started/admin-portal/accessing-and-navigating-security-operations-secops-platform/account%20settings.png)
[]The SecOps platform has the following items in the top navigation bar:
- [Search](#search)
- [Apps](#apps)
- [Explore](#explore)
- [Configure](#configure)
- [Knowledge Center](#kb-portal)
- [Support](#support-portal)
- [Profile Menu](#profile-menu)
You can click the Zscaler logo in the top left of the navigation bar to return to the main dashboard.
The left-side navigation changes dynamically based on your current location within the platform, and can be collapsed or expanded. To set the mode, hover over the menu and click the arrow icon.
![secops platform top nav bar menus](/downloads/uvm/getting-started/admin-portal/accessing-and-navigating-security-operations-secops-platform/secops%20platform%20top%20nav%20bar%20menus%20with%20callouts.png)
[]You can search the platform to locate pages, features, or tools within the SecOps platform. Press `Ctrl+K` (Windows) or `Cmd+K` (Mac) to open the search bar, then enter keywords to find relevant results.
