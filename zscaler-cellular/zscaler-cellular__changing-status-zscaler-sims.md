# Changing the Status of Zscaler SIMs
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zscaler-cellular/changing-status-zscaler-sims
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1519086.pdf

You can modify the status of the Zscaler SIMs—both physical SIMs and eSIMs—provisioned to your organization from the SIMs page.
To change the status of Zscaler SIMs:
-
In the left-side navigation, select **SIMs**.
The SIMs page appears.
- On the SIMs page, you can:
- [Modify the status of a single SIM](#modify-status-single-sim)
- [Modify the status of multiple SIMs](#modify-status-bulk)
- []Locate and select the SIM card whose status you want to modify.
-
In the **Manage **column, click the **Edit **icon.
[See image.](#edit-option-single-sim)
Alternatively, you can go to the **SIM** **Details **page by clicking the **ICCID **link and select **Update Status **from the **Update **drop-down menu in the top-right corner.
[See image.](#update-status-sim-details-page)
The Update Status window appears.
-
In the Update Status window:
-
Select a status you want to assign to the SIMs, either **Active **or **Inactive**. For example, if you want to inactivate your active SIMS, select **Inactive**. If you want to activate your inactive SIMs, select **Active**.
[See image.](#update-status-single-sim)
- Click **Save**.
The SIMs are activated or deactivated based on your chosen option.
-
[]Locate and select the SIM cards whose status you want to modify.
[See image.](#select-multiple-sims)
The SIM cards you select must be of the same status.
-
Click **Update **and select **Update Status**.
[See image.](#update-option-multiple-sims)
The Update Status window appears.
-
In the Update Status window:
-
Select a status you want to assign to the SIMs, either **Active **or **Inactive**. For example, if you want to inactivate your active SIMS, select **Inactive**. If you want to activate your inactive SIMs, select **Active**.
[See image.](#update-status-multiple-sims)
- Click **Save**.
The SIMs are activated or deactivated based on your chosen option.
