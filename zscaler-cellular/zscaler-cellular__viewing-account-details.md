# Viewing Account Details
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zscaler-cellular/viewing-account-details
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1518131.pdf

You can view your account details and configure remote access for Zscaler Support from the Customer Info page.
Viewing Account Details
To view your account details:
-
In the left-side navigation, select **Account Details.**
The **Account Details **page appears.
-
On the **Account Details **page, you can see:
- **Name**: The name of your organization.
- **Email**: The email address of the administrator associated with your organization.
- **ZIA Cloud**: The Zscaler Internet Access (ZIA) cloud associated with your organization.
- **ZIA Org ID**: The organization ID associated with the ZIA tenant for your organization.
- **ZPA Cloud**: The Zscaler Private Access (ZPA) cloud associated with your organization.
- **ZPA Org ID**: The organization ID associated with the ZPA tenant for your organization.
[See image.](#viewing-account-details)
Enabling Remote Access
To enable remote access for Zscaler Support team members:
-
On the left-side navigation, select **Account Details.**
The **Account Details **page appears.
-
On the **Account Details **page, click the arrow on **Name **field, and select **Enable Remote Access**.
[See image.](#enabling-remote-access)
The remote access support is now enabled. The members of Zscaler Support team can access the Zscaler Cellular Admin Portal for troubleshooting issues.
If you no longer need remote access support, you can unselect **Enable Remote Access**.
