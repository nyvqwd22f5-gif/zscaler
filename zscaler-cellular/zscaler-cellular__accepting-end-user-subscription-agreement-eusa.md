# Accepting the End User Subscription Agreement (EUSA)
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zscaler-cellular/accepting-end-user-subscription-agreement-eusa
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1519091.pdf

The first time you log in to the Zscaler Cellular Admin Portal, the End User Subscription Agreement (EUSA) appears. Click the notification to view the EUSA. To accept the EUSA, click **Accept**. If you don't accept the EUSA, you are logged out of the Zscaler Cellular Admin Portal. The EUSA appears every time you log in until you accept it.
After you accept it, the notification disappears.
