# Scheduling Periodic Software Updates for an App Connector Group
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/unified/scheduling-periodic-software-updates-app-connector-group
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1496231.pdf

To schedule a periodic software update for an App Connector group:
- Go to the **App Connector Groups page **(Infrastructure > Private Applications > Components > App Connector Groups).
- In the table, locate the App Connector group and click the **Edit **icon.
-
In the **Edit App Connector Group** window, under **App Connector Software Update Schedule**, choose the day of the week and start time for the periodic update.
[See Image](#update-schedule)
- Click **Save**.
To confirm that the update was scheduled, go to the [App Connector Groups](/unified/about-app-connector-groups) page and verify that the proper day and time is displayed in the **Next Periodic Software Update** column.
