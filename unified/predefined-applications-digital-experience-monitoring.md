# Predefined Applications for Digital Experience Monitoring
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/unified/predefined-applications-digital-experience-monitoring
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1492681.pdf

Predefined applications are available in the Admin Portal when you log in. The predefined applications provide quick and seamless application onboarding for admins.
To find the list of predefined applications in the Admin Portal, go to **Policies** > **Digital Experience Monitoring** > **Configuration** > **Applications**.
Your available predefined applications depend upon your particular Digital Experience Monitoring subscription:
- [Standard Subscription](#standard-plan)
- [Microsoft 365 Subscription](#m365-plan)
- [Advanced Subscription](#advanced-plan)
- [Advanced Plus Subscription](#advanced-plus-plan)
