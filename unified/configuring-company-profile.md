# Configuring the Company Profile
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zpa/configuring-company-profile
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1483476.pdf

To configure your organization's profile for ZPA:
- Go to **Configuration & Control **> **Administration Control **> **Company**.
- On the **Company Profile** page:
- **Logo**: Click **Upload File** to upload your organization's logo. You can upload any valid image file type that is 300x150 pixels or smaller. Your organization's logo is shown in the user portal and Privileged Remote Access (PRA) Portal. To learn more, see [About User Portals](/zpa/about-user-portals) and [Accessing a Privileged Remote Access Portal](/zpa/accessing-privileged-remote-access-portal).
- **Name**: Enter the name of your organization. The name cannot contain special characters, with the exception of periods (.), hyphens (-), and underscores ( _ ).
- **ZPA tenant ID**: The ID for your organizationâ€™s ZPA tenant. This field cannot be edited.
- **Favicon**: Click **Upload File** to upload your organization's favicon. You can upload an image with a valid image file type that is 32x32 pixels or smaller. After the favicon is successfully uploaded, it appears on the page. The favicon appears as the icon on a user portal browser tab, the PRA Portal browser tab, and privileged console browser tabs.
![Configuring the Company Profile](/downloads/zpa/administration/configuring-company-profile/zpa-configuring-the-company-profile.png)
- Click **Save**.
