# Editing VPN Service Edges
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/unified/editing-vpn-service-edges
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1532359.pdf

This article describes how to edit a VPN Service Edge. To learn about configuring a VPN Service Edge and the available options, see [About VPN Service Edges](/unified/about-vpn-service-edges).
To edit a VPN Service Edge:
- Go to **Infrastructure **> **Private Access** > **Component **> **VPN (for Legacy Apps) **> **VPN Service Edges**.
-
In the table, locate the VPN Service Edge you want to modify and click the **Edit **icon.
The **Edit VPN Service Edge **drawer appears.
- In the **Edit VPN Service Edge** drawer, you can only modify the following fields:
- **Name**: Update the name of the VPN Service Edge. The name cannot contain special characters, with the exception of periods (.), hyphens (-), and underscores ( _).
- **Region**: Update the region that is designated for the VPN Service Edge.
- **Client IP Pool**: Update the IP pool assigned to the VPN Service Edge. This IP pool is used with Zscaler Client Connector.
![Edit VPN Service Edge window](/downloads/zpa/vpn-legacy-apps/vpn-configuration/editing-vpn-service-edges/zpa-vpn-edit-service-edge.png)
- Click **Save**.
