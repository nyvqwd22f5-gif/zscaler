# Understanding Administrator Management Settings
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/unified/understanding-administrator-management-settings
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1488256.pdf

The Administrator Management page (Administration > Admin Management > Administrator Management > Internet Access Administrators > Administrator Management) leads you to [ZIdentity](/zidentity/what-zidentity), the area of the Admin Portal where you can manage admins for your organization.
