# Viewing the Cybersecurity Dashboard
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/unified/viewing-cybersecurity-dashboard
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1497896.pdf

The Cybersecurity dashboard provides information about malicious activity that affects your organization.
Filtering
**Time Range**: Use the Time Range filter in the upper right to choose a specific time range between **1 Day** and **90 Days** in which to view data. The selected period applies to all data within the dashboard. The default time range is **14 Days**.
Dashboard Widgets
- [Your Cybersecurity Transactions from Last ___ Days](#transactions)
- [Advanced Threat Categories](#advanced-threats)
- [Top Threat Locations](#top-locations)
- [Sandbox Threats](#sandbox-threats)
- [Your SSL Inspection Review](#ssl-inspection)
[]This widget shows the cybersecurity transactions detected and blocked by Zscaler during the selected time period. Click the toggle to switch between **Malicious Transactions** and **Policy Blocks**.
Click **View Transaction Activity **to see more detail on the [Transactional Activity Dashboard](/unified/viewing-transactional-activity-dashboard).
[See image.](#transactions-image)
[]This widget shows the top threat categories stopped by Zscaler. Click **View All Advanced Threats **to see all advanced threat activity on the [Advanced Threats Dashboard](/unified/viewing-advanced-threats-dashboard).
[See image.](#advanced-threats-image)
[]This widget shows the countries where threats are originating. Click **View All Threat Locations **to see more detail on the [Threat Locations Dashboard](/unified/viewing-threat-locations-dashboard).
[See image.](#top-locations-image)
[]This widget shows the flow and dispensation of files sent to the sandbox. Click **View All Sandbox Threats **to see more detail on the [Sandbox Threats Dashboard](/unified/viewing-sandbox-threats-dashboard).
[See image.](#sandbox-threats-image)
[]This widget shows the SSL traffic that occurred and that was inspected. Click View SSL Inspection to see more detail on the [SSL Inspection Dashboard](/unified/viewing-ssl-inspection-dashboard).
