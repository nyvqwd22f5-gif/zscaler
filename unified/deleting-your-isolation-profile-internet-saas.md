# Deleting Your Isolation Profile for Internet & SaaS
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/unified/deleting-your-isolation-profile-internet-saas
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1492791.pdf

After [creating an isolation profile for Internet & SaaS](/unified/creating-isolation-profiles-internet-saas), you can [edit](/unified/editing-your-isolation-profile-internet-saas) or delete the profile at any time.
[Default isolation profiles](/unified/default-isolation-profiles-isolation) cannot be deleted.
To delete your Internet & SaaS isolation profile:
- Go to **Policies **> **Common Configuration **> **Resources** > **Browser** **Isolation**.
The **Isolation Profiles** page appears.
- Click the **Edit** icon for the chosen profile.
The **Edit Isolation Profile** window appears.
- Click **Delete**.
[See image.](#Edit-Iso-Profile_Delete-button)
- The **Delete Isolation Profile** window appears and asks you to confirm. Click **Delete**.
[See image.](#Delete-isolation-profile-confirm-window)
- The page refreshes, and a temporary message appears to confirm the isolation profile has been deleted.
