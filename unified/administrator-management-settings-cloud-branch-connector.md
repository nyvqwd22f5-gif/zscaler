# Administrator Management Settings for Cloud & Branch Connector
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/unified/administrator-management-settings-cloud-branch-connector
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1520346.pdf

The Administrator Management page (Administration > Admin Management > Administrator Management > Branch and Cloud Connector > Administrators Management) leads you to [ZIdentity](/zidentity/what-zidentity), the area of the Admin Portal where you can manage admins for your organization.
![Link to ZIdentity Administration from Cloud and Branch Connector Administrator Management page](/downloads/unified/administration/internet-saas/admin-user-role-management/understanding-administrator-management-settings/ZIdentityAdmin.png)
To learn more, see [ZIdentity Administration](/zidentity/administration).
