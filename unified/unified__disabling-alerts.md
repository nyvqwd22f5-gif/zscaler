# Disabling Alerts
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/unified/disabling-alerts
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1495151.pdf

[]To disable alerts:
- Go to **Administration **>** Alerts **> **Platform Alerts**.
-
Click the** Edit** icon next to the alert you want to disable.
The **Edit Alert Definition** window appears.
- Under **Status**, choose **Disabled** from the drop-down menu.
- Click **Save** and activate the change.
To learn more about alerts, see [About Alerts](/unified/about-alerts-0).
