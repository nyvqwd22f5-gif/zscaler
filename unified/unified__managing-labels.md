# Managing Labels
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/unified/managing-labels
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1491781.pdf

After you have acquired the full permission for Alerts, you can manage your labels and access the Labels page (**Policies **> **Digital Experience Monitoring** > **Labels**).
To manage your labels, you can:
- [Add a new label.](#addlabel)
- [View the label.](#viewlabel)
- [Edit the label.](#editlabel)
- [Delete the label.](#deletelabel)
[]
- Go to one of the following to add a label:
-
**Labels** > **Add New Label**
[See image.](#LabelsAddNewLabel)
-
**Administration** > **Alerts** > **Rules** > **Add Alert Rule** > **Labels (Optional)** > **Add New Label**
[See image.](#AddNewAlertRuleLabels)
- In the **Create New Label** window:
- **Name**: Enter the name of the label.
-
**Description** (Optional): Enter the description of the label.
The Label Name and Description can each consist of a maximum 120 alphanumeric and the following special characters: hyphen (-), space ( ), or underscore (_).
[See image.](#CreateNewLabelWindow)
- Click **Save**.
You can assign the label to an alert rule in either the** Add New Alert Rule** or **Edit Alert Rule** window. To learn more, see [Configuring an Alert Rule](/unified/configuring-alert-rule) and [Editing an Alert Rule](/unified/editing-alert-rule).
[]On the Labels page:
- Click the **View **icon on your selected label.
- In the **View Label** window, you can view:
- **Name**: The label name.
-
**Description**: The label description.
[See image.](#ViewLabelWindow)
- Click **Cancel**.
[]On the Labels page:
- Click the **Edit **icon on your selected label.
- In the **Edit Label** window:
- **Name**: Enter the name of the label.
-
**Description** (Optional): Enter the description of the label.
The Label Name and Description can each consist of a maximum 120 alphanumeric and the following special characters: hyphen (-), space ( ), or underscore (_).
[See image.](#EditLabelWindow)
- Click **Save**.
You cannot delete a label if an alert rule is tagged with it.
[]On the Labels page:
- Click the **Delete **icon on your selected label.
-
Click **Delete** in the **Delete ****<**Label> window. A confirmation on the Labels page appears briefly.
[See image.](#DeleteLabelWindow)
