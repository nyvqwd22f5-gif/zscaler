# Deleting Backups for Private Applications
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/unified/deleting-backups-private-applications
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1520751.pdf

To delete a [backup](/unified/about-backup-and-restore-private-applications):
- Go to **Administration** > **Backup & Restore** > **Private Applications**.
-
Locate the **Backup Name** within the table and click the **Delete **(![zpa-delete-icon.png](/downloads/zpa/administration/backup-restore/deleting-restore-points/zpa-delete-icon.png)
) icon.
The **Delete Backup **window appears.
![Delete Backup window](/downloads/zpa/administration/backup-restore/deleting-restore-points/zpa-delete-backup.png)
- Click **Delete**.
Deleting a backup also deletes the linked [restore activities](/unified/viewing-restore-activities-and-restore-reports-private-applications). For example, after deleting a backup, information about who restored the backup and the restore reports are deleted.
