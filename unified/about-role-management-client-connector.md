# About Zscaler Client Connector Role Management
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/unified/about-role-management-client-connector
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1490851.pdf

You can create roles to control access to Admin Portal settings and assign these roles to Internet & SaaS, Private Applications, and Digital Experience Monitoring administrators.
About the Role Management Page
On the Role Management page (Administration > Admin Management > Role Based Access Control > Client Connector), you can:
- [Add a role](/unified/adding-roles).
- View the following information for roles:
- **Role Name**: Name of role created by the super admin.
- **Full Access**: Settings that the role has access to configure.
- **View-Only Access**: Settings that the role can only view.
- Edit a role.
- Delete a role.
- View a role.
- Go to the [Administrators page](/unified/about-administration-management).
