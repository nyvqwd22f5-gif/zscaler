# Reviewing Approvals in the PRA Portal
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/unified/reviewing-approvals-pra-portal
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1525476.pdf

After the admin has enabled privileged approvals in a privileged portal policy, you can access the [My Approvals page](/unified/about-my-approvals) in the PRA Portal. You can review, approve, and reject requested approvals.
[]Approving an Approval Request
To approve a privileged approval request:
- Click the **My Approvals** icon on the [PRA Portal home page](/unified/accessing-privileged-remote-access-portal).
- Click the **My Approvals** tab.
- Click the **Approve** icon next to the approval request.
The **Approve Request** window appears.
- In the **Approve Request** window:
- Review the approval request information.
- Enable **Modify Time** if you want to adjust the time window of the request.
- (Optional) You can include a message to the requester.
[See image.](#approve)
- Click **Approve**.
The approval request is displayed as Approved on the [My Approvals](/unified/about-my-approvals) page.
[]Rejecting an Approval Request
To reject an approval request:
- Click the **My Approvals** icon on the [PRA Portal home page](/unified/accessing-privileged-remote-access-portal).
- Click the **My Approvals** tab.
- Click the **Reject** icon next to the approval request.
The **Reject Request** window appears.
- In the **Reject Request** window:
- Review the approval request information.
- (Optional) You can include a message to the requester.
[See image.](#reject)
- Click **Reject**.
The approval request is displayed as Rejected on the [My Approvals](/unified/about-my-approvals) page.
