# Adding Alert Subscriptions
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/unified/adding-alert-subscriptions
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1495136.pdf

To subscribe and receive the alerts through email:
- Go to **Administration **>** Alerts **> **Platform Alerts**.
- Click the **Publish Alerts** tab.
-
Click **Add Alert Subscription**.
The **Add Alert Subscription** window appears.
- In the **Add Alert Subscription** window:
- **Email**: Enter a valid email address.
- **Description**: (Optional) Enter any additional comments or information. The description cannot exceed 10,240 characters.
- For each [alert class](/unified/about-alerts-0), select the severity level of the alerts that the recipient receives.
- Click **Save** and activate the change.
