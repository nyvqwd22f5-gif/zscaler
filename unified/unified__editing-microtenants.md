# Editing Microtenants
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/unified/editing-microtenants
PDF: https://help.zscaler.us/pdf/com/en/1491601.pdf

To edit a Microtenant:
- Go to **Private App Microtenants** (Administration > Role Based Access Control > Private App Microtenants).
- In the table, locate the Microtenant you want to modify and click the **Edit **icon.
The **Edit Microtenant** window appears.
- In the **Edit Microtenant** window, you can modify any field.
![Edit Microtenant window](/downloads/unified/administration/private-applications/admin-user-role-management/delegated-tenant-administration/editing-microtenants/edit-microtenant-window.png)
- Click **Save**.
