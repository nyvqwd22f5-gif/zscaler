# Enabling Device Groups for Android
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/unified/enabling-device-groups-android
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1532396.pdf

To [create device groups](/unified/creating-device-groups) for Android, you must first enable Use Device Groups in Service Entitlement on the Android tab in [Platform Settings](/unified/about-platform-settings).
This option applies to Zscaler Client Connector version 3.7 and later for Android.
To enable **Use Device Groups in Service Entitlement**:
- In the Admin Portal, go to Infrastructure > Connectors > Client > Android.
- Click the **Platform Settings** tab.
- Enable **Use Device Groups in Service Entitlement**.
