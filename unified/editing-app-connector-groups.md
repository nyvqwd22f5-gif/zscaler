# Editing App Connector Groups
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/unified/editing-app-connector-groups
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1496066.pdf

To edit a configured App Connector group:
- Go to the **App Connector Groups** page (Infrastructure > Private Access > Component > App Connector Groups).
-
In the table, locate the App Connector group you want to modify and click the **Edit **icon.
App Connector groups that are managed by Zscaler are read only and cannot be edited.
The **Edit Connector Group** window appears.
- In the **Edit App Connector Group** window, you can modify any field.
![Editing an App Connector Group ](/downloads/zpa/app-connector-management/app-connector-groups/editing-connector-groups/Updatededitedappconngroup.png)
- Click **Save**.
