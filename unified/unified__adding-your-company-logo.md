# Adding Your Company Logo to User Notifications, Emails, and Banners
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/unified/adding-your-company-logo
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1488046.pdf

You can customize the Zscaler interface to include your logo in application banners, notifications, and emails sent to your users regarding security issues.
To add a logo:
- On the Miscellaneous page, under **Company Logo**, click **Upload File**.
- Upload the logo from your local directory. Zscaler supports logos with the following specifications:
- Dimensions of 300 x 220 pixels or less
- Size of 10 kB or less
- .png, .jpg, or .gif file types
- Click **Save**.
- Click **Next **to [review and activate policies](/unified/review-and-activate-policies) and complete your setup of Zscaler.
