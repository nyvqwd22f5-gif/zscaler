# Customizing Zscaler Client Connector with Install Options for iOS
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/unified/customizing-zscaler-client-connector-install-options-ios
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1491966.pdf

You can customize and deploy Zscaler Client Connector to your users' devices by adding the app to the Mobile Device Management (MDM) used by your organization. The steps for downloading and deploying the app depends upon your MDM.
To install Zscaler Client Connector app for your iOS devices, choose any one of the following deployment procedures:
- [Deploying Zscaler Client Connector with Microsoft Intune for iOS](/unified/deploying-zscaler-client-connector-microsoft-intune-ios)
- [Deploying Zscaler Client Connector with Jamf Pro for iOS](/unified/deploying-zscaler-client-connector-jamf-pro-ios)
- [Deploying Zscaler Client Connector with Workspace ONE UEM for iOS](/unified/deploying-zscaler-client-connector-workspace-one-uem-ios)
- [Deploying Zscaler Client Connector with MobileIron for iOS](/unified/deploying-zscaler-client-connector-mobileiron-ios)
- [Deploying Zscaler Client Connector with MaaS360 for iOS](/unified/deploying-zscaler-client-connector-maas360-ios)
