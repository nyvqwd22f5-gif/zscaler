# Customizing Zscaler Client Connector with Install Options for Android
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/unified/customizing-zscaler-client-connector-install-options-android
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1491936.pdf

You can customize and deploy Zscaler Client Connector to your users' devices by adding the app to the Mobile Device Management (MDM) used by your organization. The steps for downloading and deploying the app depends upon your MDM.
To install Zscaler Client Connector on your Android devices, choose any one of the following deployment procedures:
- [Deploying Zscaler Client Connector with MaaS360 for Android](/unified/deploying-zscaler-client-connector-maas360-android)
- [Deploying Zscaler Client Connector with Microsoft Intune for Android](/unified/deploying-zscaler-client-connector-microsoft-intune-android)
- [Deploying Zscaler Client Connector with MobileIron for Android](/unified/deploying-zscaler-client-connector-mobileiron-android)
- [Deploying Zscaler Client Connector with Workspace ONE UEM for Android](/unified/deploying-zscaler-client-connector-workspace-one-uem-android)
