# Mapping Departments
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/business-insights/mapping-departments
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1507971.pdf

You can map departments to various divisions based on your requirements. You can add up to 32 divisions.
To map departments:
- Go to **Workplace **> **Workplace** **Settings **> **Department Mapping**.
-
Click **Map Departments**.
The **Map Departments** page appears.
- On the **Map Departments **page:
- Enter a name for the division in the first field.
-
Select the departments that you want to map to the division in the next field.
[See image.](#map)
Click **Add Division** to map more departments.
-
Click **Save**.
The departments are mapped to the division. You can start viewing workplace metrics based on the division.
