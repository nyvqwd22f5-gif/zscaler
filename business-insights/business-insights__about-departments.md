# About Departments
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/business-insights/about-departments
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1508376.pdf

The Departments page shows the users' department data synced from your Zscaler Internet Access (ZIA) tenant. Any department configuration or changes that you make on the [User Management](/zia/authentication-administration/provisioning-authenticating-users/user-management) pages in the ZIA Admin Portal are synced to this page.
The Departments page provides the following benefits and allows you to:
- View the departments configured in your ZIA tenant.
- View the list of departments that are computed for Business Insights.
About the Department Page
On the Departments page (Administration > ZIA Users > Departments), you can do the following:
- Search for a department.
- View a list of configured departments. For each department, you can view:
- **Name**: The name of the department.
- **Comments**: The comments regarding the department, if available.
- [Modify the table and its columns](/business-insights/using-tables-business-insights).
- Go to the [ZIA Users](/business-insights/about-zia-users) page.
