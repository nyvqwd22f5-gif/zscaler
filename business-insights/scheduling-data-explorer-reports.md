# Scheduling Data Explorer Reports
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/business-insights/scheduling-data-explorer-reports
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1532021.pdf

You can schedule Data Explorer reports for your customized views.
To schedule a report:
- Go to **Analytics **> **Data** **Explorer**.
-
[Configure a Data Explorer view](/business-insights/configuring-data-explorer-views). You can schedule Data Explorer reports only when the time period selected is **Last Day**, **Last Week**, or **Last Month**.
The **Save Report **window appears.
-
In the **Save Report** window:
- **Report Name**: Enter a name for the scheduled report.
- **Schedule Report**: Enable to activate the report.
- **Deliver**: Select the report generation frequency (i.e., **Daily** or **Weekly**).
- **Email Recipients**: Enter the email addresses of the users who must receive the report. Use a comma to separate multiple email addresses.
- Click **Save and Schedule**.
[See image.](#schedule-explorer-report)
The report is successfully scheduled. You can view and manage the scheduled reports on the [Data Explorer Reports](/business-insights/about-data-explorer-reports) page.
