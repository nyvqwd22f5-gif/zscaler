# Editing or Deleting Items in Business Insights
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/business-insights/editing-or-deleting-items-business-insights
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1473121.pdf

In the Business Insights Admin Portal, you can edit or delete items you've configured, such as admins and roles.
Editing Items
To edit items:
- Navigate to the item you want to edit. In this example, it's an office.
- Click the **Edit** icon for that item.
- Make your edits and save the changes.
[See image.](#edit-img)
[]![Edit Icon](/downloads/business-insights/getting-started/admin-portal/editing-or-deleting-items-business-insights/Business%3D-Insights-edit-icon.png)
Deleting Items
To delete items:
- Navigate to the item you want to delete. In this example, it's an office.
-
Click the **Delete** icon for that item.
[See image.](#delete-img)
-
A confirmation message appears. If you are sure you want to delete the item, click **Delete**.
