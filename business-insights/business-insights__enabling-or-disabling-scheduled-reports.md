# Enabling or Disabling Scheduled Reports
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/business-insights/enabling-or-disabling-scheduled-reports
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1498386.pdf

You can enable or disable scheduled reports based on your organization's requirements. Disabling a scheduled report stops report generation and distribution to the recipients and vice versa when you enable a report.
Enabling a Scheduled Report
To enable a scheduled report:
- Go to **Analytics** > **Reports **> **Application Reports **or** Workplace Reports**.
- Locate the report you want to enable.
-
Select the **Enabled** option for the report.
[See image.](#enable)
The scheduled report is successfully enabled and resumes report distribution per the configuration.
Disabling a Scheduled Report
To disable a scheduled report:
- Go to **Analytics** > **Reports **> **Application Reports **or** Workplace Reports**.
- Locate the report you want to disable.
-
Deselect the **Enabled** option for the report.
[See image.](#disable)
The scheduled report is successfully disabled and halts report distribution until enabled.
