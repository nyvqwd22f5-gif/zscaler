# About Zscaler Client Connector IdP
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zscaler-client-connector/about-zscaler-app-idp
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1317676.pdf

The Zscaler Client Connector Portal can function as an identity provider (IdP) for the Zscaler service. With this feature, users do not need to be tied to your organizationâ€™s standard identity provider (IdP) in order to authenticate to the Zscaler service. Instead, if your organization uses SAML-based single sign-on (SSO), the app can use a device token to auto-provision and silently authenticate users and devices for the Zscaler service.
To learn more, see [Using Zscaler Client Connector Portal as an Identity Provider (IdP)](/zscaler-client-connector/using-zscaler-app-portal-identity-provider-idp).
