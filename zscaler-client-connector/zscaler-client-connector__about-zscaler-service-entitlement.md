# About Zscaler Service Entitlement
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zscaler-client-connector/about-zscaler-service-entitlement
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1328951.pdf

Zscaler Client Connector provides users access to Zscaler Internet Access (ZIA), Zscaler Private Access (ZPA), Zscaler Digital Experience (ZDX), and Zscaler Deception services. Zscaler Service Entitlement allows you to select which group of users or device groups can enroll into the ZPA, ZDX, or Zscaler Deception services. By default, ZIA enrollment is always enabled for all users.
Service entitlements provide the following benefits and allow you to:
- Assign users and device groups to the subscribed Zscaler services.
- Control which users or device groups within your organization are assigned to a Zscaler service.
About the Zscaler Service Entitlement Page
On the Zscaler Service Entitlement page, (Administration > Zscaler Service Entitlement), you can do the following:
- [Enable ZDX for all users.](/zscaler-client-connector/enabling-zdx-all-users)
- [Enable ZDX for a group of users](/zscaler-client-connector/selective-entitlement-enabling-zdx-group-users).
- [Enable ZDX for device groups](/zscaler-client-connector/enabling-zdx-device-groups).
- [Enable ZIA for device groups](/zscaler-client-connector/enabling-zia-device-groups).
- [Enable Zscaler Deception for a group of users](/zscaler-client-connector/selective-entitlement-enabling-deception-group-users).
- [Enable Zscaler Deception for device groups](/zscaler-client-connector/enabling-zscaler-deception-device-groups).
- [Enable ZPA for a group of users](/zscaler-client-connector/selective-entitlement-enabling-zpa-group-users).
- [Enable ZPA for device groups](/zscaler-client-connector/enabling-zpa-device-groups).
- [Configure ZPA machine tunnel for all devices](/zscaler-client-connector/selective-entitlement-configuring-zpa-machine-tunnel-all).
