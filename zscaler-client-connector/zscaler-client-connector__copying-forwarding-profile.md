# Copying a Forwarding Profile
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zscaler-client-connector/copying-forwarding-profile
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1470366.pdf

You can copy an existing Zscaler Client Connector Forwarding Profile and customize the profile to fit your needs, instead of having to configure each setting in a new profile.
Follow these steps to copy a Zscaler Client Connector Forwarding Profile:
You cannot copy the default profile.
- In the Zscaler Client Connector Portal, go to **Administration**.
- In the left-side navigation, go to **Forwarding Profile**.
- Click the **Copy **icon next to the profile you want to copy.
[See Image](#copy)
- In the **Copy Forwarding Profile **window:
- Enter a new name in the **Profile Name **field.
- Change the settings as desired.
- Click **Save**.
[See Image](#copy-window)
Your copied profile appears at the bottom of the Forwarding Profile table.
You can copy a maximum of 10 profiles at the same time.
