# Self Service Notifications in Zscaler Client Connector
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zscaler-client-connector/self-service-notifications-zscaler-client-connector
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1473946.pdf

After the admins have [configured the Self Service settings](/zdx/configuring-self-service-settings), users receive notifications when issues are detected and need attention. Each notification contains a brief diagnosis and recommendation that might resolve the CPU or Wi-Fi issue.
For the minimum required version compatibility for Self Service, see [Supported Versions & Feature Compatibility](/zdx/supported-versions-feature-compatibility).
Enabling and Disabling Notifications in Zscaler Client Connector
In Zscaler Client Connector, you can enable or disable the Self Service notifications:
- From the left-side navigation, click **More**.
- Under **Settings**, enable the toggle for **Show Self Service notification popups**.
![Self Service notification settings on Zscaler Client Connector](/downloads/client-connector/end-user-guide/self-service-notifications-zscaler-client-connector/self-service-ZClientconnector.png)
