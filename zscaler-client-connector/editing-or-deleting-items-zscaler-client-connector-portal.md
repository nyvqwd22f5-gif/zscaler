# Editing or Deleting Items in the Zscaler Client Connector Portal
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zscaler-client-connector/editing-or-deleting-items-zscaler-client-connector-portal
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1328901.pdf

In the Zscaler Client Connector Portal, you may be able to edit or delete items you've configured. For example, you can edit or delete policy rules you've configured in the Zscaler Client Connector Portal.
Editing Items
To edit items:
- Go to the item you want to edit.
- Click the **Edit** icon.
![The Edit icon in the Zscaler Client Connector Portal](/downloads/zia/edit-items-z-app-portal.png)
Deleting Items
To delete items:
- Go to the item you want to edit.
- Click the **Delete** icon.
![The Delete icon in the Zscaler Client Connector Portal](/downloads/zia/delete-items-z-app-portal.png)
