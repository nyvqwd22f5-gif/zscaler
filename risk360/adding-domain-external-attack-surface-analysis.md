# Adding a Domain for External Attack Surface Analysis
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/unified/adding-domain-external-attack-surface-analysis
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1530709.pdf

You can add domains on the Seeds Management page to run frequent scans on them to detect vulnerabilities for external attack surfaces. The Risk360 service then quantifies the risk determined on various parameters for potential breach and visualizes the risk as factors on the [Factors](/unified/viewing-risk-factors) page.
To add a domain:
- Go to **Administration **>** Admin Management **> **Administrator Management **> **Seeds Management.**
- In the **Domains** field, enter the domain that you want to scan for external attack surface analysis. You can add up to 10 domains.
-
Click **Add**.
