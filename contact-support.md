# Contacting Zscaler Support
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/contact-support

Administrators can log in to the Zscaler Admin Portal for their respective product to access the "Submit a Ticket" page and open a Zscaler Support ticket. This is the recommended and most efficient method for creating cases and obtaining support.
Additionally, the Zscaler Support Portal is accessible via the [Zenith Community](https://community.zscaler.com/zenith/s/). You can register and log in to Zenith Community using the email address registered with Zscaler to submit and manage tickets.
