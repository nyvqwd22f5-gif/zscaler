# Zscaler Service Continuity Customer Notification Protocol
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/zdx/zscaler-service-continuity-customer-notification-protocol
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1358921.pdf

![ZIA Service Continuity Matrix](/downloads/zia/getting-started/admin-portal/zscaler-service-continuity-customer-notification-protocol/zia-servicecontinuitymatrix_0.png)
Zscaler's Service Continuity Policy governs how and when we make changes to our services. Our End-of-Sale (EOS) and End-of-Life (EOL) Policy is separate from our Service Continuity Policy. To learn more, see [EOS and EOL Policy](/eos-eol/eos-and-eol-policy).
