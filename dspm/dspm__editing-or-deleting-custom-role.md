# Editing or Deleting a Custom Role
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/dspm/editing-or-deleting-custom-role
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1474996.pdf

You can edit a custom role and modify the permissions to view, access, or restrict access to specific modules.
You cannot edit or delete [predefined roles](/dspm/predefined-roles-and-permissions).
Editing a Custom Role
To edit a role:
- Go to **Administration **>** Authentication & Authorization > Access Roles**.
-
On the **Access Roles** page, click the **Edit** icon for the custom role that you want to edit.
[See image.](#editicon)
-
In the **Edit Role** window, change the **Role Name** or **Permissions**, as required.
[See image.](#editwindow)
-
Click **Update**.
A message appears indicating that the role is updated successfully.
Deleting a Custom Role
To delete a custom role:
- Go to **Administration **>** Authentication & Authorization > Access Roles**.
-
On the **Access Roles** page, click the **Delete **icon for the custom role that you want to delete.
[See image.](#deleteicon)
-
In the **Delete Role** window, click **Delete**.
[See image.](#deletewindow)
The role is deleted from the DSPM Admin Portal.
