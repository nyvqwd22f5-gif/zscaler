# Managing Compliance Frameworks
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/dspm/managing-compliance-frameworks
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1529784.pdf

You can enable or disable a predefined framework and edit, delete, enable, or disable a custom framework.
Enabling or Disabling a Compliance Framework
Failed policies are not calculated for disabled frameworks.
When you click a disabled framework, you are directed to the Configuration tab and the Summary, Policies, and Resources tabs are disabled.
To enable or disable a compliance framework:
- Go to **Analytics **> **Compliance**.
- On the **Compliance **page, click the **Actions **(**![dspm-actions-menu.jpg](/downloads/dspm/analytics/compliance/managing-compliance-frameworks/dspm-actions-menu.jpg)
**)** **icon for the required compliance framework.
-
Select **Enable **or **Disable**.
[See image.](#disablecompliance)
The disabled frameworks are displayed at the end and grayed out.
Editing a Custom Compliance Framework
To edit a custom compliance framework:
- Go to **Analytics **> **Compliance**.
- On the **Compliance **page, click the **Actions **(**![dspm-actions-menu.jpg](/downloads/dspm/analytics/compliance/managing-compliance-frameworks/dspm-actions-menu.jpg)
**)** **icon for the required compliance framework.
-
Select **Edit**.
[See image.](#editcompliance)
You are directed to the **Summary **page.
- On the **Summary **page, change the **Framework Name** or **Framework Description** as required.
-
Click the **Edit **icon for **Categories**, **Policies**,** **or **DLP Engines, **and update the fields as required.
[See image.](#summarypage)
- Click **Finish**.
Deleting a Custom Compliance Framework
To delete a custom compliance framework:
- Go to **Analytics **> **Compliance**.
- On the **Compliance **page, click the **Actions **(**![dspm-actions-menu.jpg](/downloads/dspm/analytics/compliance/managing-compliance-frameworks/dspm-actions-menu.jpg)
**) icon for the required compliance framework.
-
Select **Delete**.
[See image.](#deletecustomcompliance)
-
On the **Delete Framework** window, enter `CONFIRM`, and then click **Delete**.
The custom framework is deleted from the DSPM Admin Portal.
