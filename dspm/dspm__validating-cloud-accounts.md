# Validating Cloud Accounts
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/dspm/validating-cloud-accounts
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1520296.pdf

DSPM validates the cloud accounts every hour to check if the onboarding templates are deployed successfully and roles and permissions are configured for each account. You can also run an on-demand validation without waiting for DSPM's hourly validations.
Validating a Single Account
To validate a single account:
- Go to **Administration **> **Configuration **> **Cloud Accounts**.
- Select the cloud account.
-
Select the **Accounts **tab for AWS, **Subscriptions **tab for Azure, or **Projects **tab for GCP.
The tab name varies depending on the selected cloud account.
-
Click the **Actions **icon for the account you want to validate, and click **On Demand Validation**.
[See image.](#account-actions)
-
In the **On Demand Validation** window, click **Confirm**.
[See image.](#ondemand-validation)
Validating all Accounts in the Organization
To validate all accounts in the organization:
- Go to **Administration **> **Configuration **> **Cloud Accounts**.
- Select the cloud account.
-
Click **Manage**, and then select **On Demand Validation** from the drop-down menu.
[See image.](#manage-dropdown)
-
In the **On Demand Validation** window, click **Confirm**.
[See image.](#ondemandvalidationwindow)
