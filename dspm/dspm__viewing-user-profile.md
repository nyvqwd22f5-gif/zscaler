# Viewing the User Profile
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/dspm/viewing-user-profile
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1474746.pdf

Your account and tenant details are displayed on the My Profile page.
To view your profile:
- Click the **Profile** icon (![ds-profileicon.png](/downloads/dspm/getting-started/admin-portal/viewing-user-profile/ds-profileicon.png)
) in the left-side navigation.
- On the **My Profile** page, you can see:
- **Login ID**: Your registered login ID.
- **Role**: The role that is assigned to you.
- **My Tenants**: The **Tenant Name**, **Tenant ID**, **ZIA Tenant ID**, and **ZIA Cloud** associated with your account.
![The My Profile page in the DSPM Admin Portal shows your profile information.](/downloads/dspm/getting-started/admin-portal/viewing-user-profile/dspm-my-profile-page.png)
