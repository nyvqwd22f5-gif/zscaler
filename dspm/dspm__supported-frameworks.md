# Supported Frameworks
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/dspm/supported-frameworks
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1516676.pdf

DSPM supports many compliance frameworks across different categories, such as cybersecurity health, finance, data protection, CIS, AI benchmarks, etc.
The following CIS and compliance frameworks are supported:
