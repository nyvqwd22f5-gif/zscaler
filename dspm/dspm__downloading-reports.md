# Downloading Reports
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/dspm/downloading-reports
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1474771.pdf

You can download reports related to [alerts](/dspm/about-alerts), [data inventory](/dspm/about-data-inventory), and [policies](/dspm/about-data-posture-policies) to review and assess DSPM's performance in proactively monitoring and detecting sensitive data in your cloud resources.
To download the reports:
- Go to **Analytics** > **Report Management**.
- You can view the following details:
- **Downloads**: The name of the report.
- **Report Type**: The file format. Reports are available as Excel files only.
- **Category**: The type of report.
- **Generation Time**: The date and time when the report was generated.
- **Status**: The status of the report.
- **In Progress**: When the report is being generated.
- **Download**: When the report is ready for download.
- Click the **Download** icon for the required report and save the file on your local system.
