# Editing or Deleting a Scan Rule
Source: https://help.zscaler.com/dspm/editing-or-deleting-scan-rule
PDF: https://help.zscaler.com/pdf/com/en/1483291.pdf

You can edit or delete the scan rule as required.
Editing a Scan Rule
To edit the scan rule:
- Go to **Administration** > **Scan Settings**.
- Select the **Scan Rules **tab.
-
Do one of the following:
-
Click **Actions** (![ds-actionsicon.png](/downloads/dspm/scan-settings/editing-scan-settings/ds-actionsicon.png)
), and select **Edit** for the required scan rule.
[See image.](#ds-action-edit)
-
Click the name of the scan rule to go to the [scan rule details](/dspm/about-scan-settings#scan_settings) page, then click the **Edit** icon.
[See image.](#ds-details-edit)
The **Edit Scan Rules** page appears.
- On the **Edit Scan Rules** page, modify the configuration as required. To learn more about scan rules, see [About Scan Settings](/dspm/about-scan-settings#scan_settings).
-
Review the changes, then click **Finish**.
A message appears indicating that the scan setting is updated.
Deleting a Scan Rule
To delete the scan rule:
- Go to **Administration** > **Scan Settings**.
- Select the **Scan Rules **tab.
-
Do one of the following:
-
Click **Actions** (![ds-actionsicon.png](/downloads/dspm/scan-settings/editing-scan-settings/ds-actionsicon.png)
), and select **Delete **for the required scan rule.
[See image.](#ds-action-delete)
-
Click the name of the scan rule to go to the [scan rule details](/dspm/about-scan-settings#scan_settings) page, then click the **Delete **icon.
[See image.](#ds-details-delete)
The **Delete Scan Rule** confirmation appears.
-
In the **Delete Scan Rule** confirmation window, click **Confirm**.
[See image.](#ds-delete-window)
The scan rule is deleted from the DSPM Admin Portal.
